1The Districts Act, 1836

( ACT NO. XXI OF 1836 )

[ 11th September, 1836 ]

 
 
Power to create new Districts
It shall be lawful for the 2[***] Government, by notification in the official Gazette, to create new districts in any part of Bangladesh.
 
 
3[Government may, from time to time, alter the limits of the existing districts in any part of Bangladesh].
