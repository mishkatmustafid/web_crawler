1. Local Syndicated Loan Agreement

Section/Paragraph Comments
Clause 1.1 – Definition of “Letter of Continuation” Please delete the words “and set off” after the word “Letter of Continuation”.
Clause 6.1 – Definition of “Statutory Liquidity Requirement” In the definition of “Statutory Liquidity Requirement” in Clause 6.1, after “20.1” please insert “per cent”.
