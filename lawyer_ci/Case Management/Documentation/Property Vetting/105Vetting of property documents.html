8. Chain of Ownership : Without perusing C.S. Khatian No. 104 & 56, S.A. Khatian No. 191 & 125 and R.S. Khatian No. 435, 92 & 229 and deed of sale No. 6017 dated 30.04.1969, 1677 dated 25.02.1976, 3957 dated 25.07.1977 & 2796 dated 04.06.1977, it is difficult to ascertain  complete chain of ownership of the property.

However, upon perusal of Title Deed No. 7318 dated 11.04.2005, it appears that Jagot Chandra Mondal became owner  of some land by way of succession from his father Sree Kishor Mondal. But  no deed/documents has been referred to us so as to determine how Sree Kishor Mondal became owner  of  the said land. It also appears that  in the above Deed Sree Jagot Chandra Mondal   has been shown as a S.A. Tenant.

After, death of Sree Jagot Chandra Mondal, his legal heirs Sree Charan Mondal and Gonga Charan Mondal became owners  of the land by way of succession. It also appears from the above Deed that their name have been recorded in R.S. operation.

Subsequently, Sree Charan Mondal and Gonga Charan Mondal sold the said land to Mr. Wahidul Haque vide Deed of Sale No. 6017 dated 30.04.1969. Thereafter, Mr. Wahidul Haque sold the said land to Mr. Syed Ashraful Hossain vide deed of Sale No. 1677 dated 25.02.1976.

Thereafter, Mrs. Hasnin Kibria Sonia and others became owners some land by way of purchase from Mr. Syed Wahidul Haque vide deed of Sale No. 3957 dated 25.07.1977.

Upon perusal of Deed of Sale No. 7318 dated 11.04.2005 and 18026 dated 25.07.2005, it appears that thereafter Mr.  Md. Idris  Ali Dewan  became the owner of some land by way of purchase  through  the Court of 5th Sub-judge, Dhaka vide Deed of Sale No. 16668, dated 15.01.69.  In the above Deed of Sale Md. Idris Ali Dewan was also shown as a R.S. Tenant. Corresponding R.S. khatians has not been submitted to us as such we could not determine the matter.

We have perused the Deed of Sale No. 16668 and found that Mr. Idris Ali Dewan was not a party to that agreement and the  property transacted under Deed of Sale No. 16668  does not match with the property in question. The Bank should ask the borrower to clarify the matter.

Mrs. Hasnin Kibria Sonia, Mr. Wahabuzzaman and others became owner  of said land by way of purchase from Md. Idris vide Deed of Sale No. 2796 dated 04.06.1977.

Please note that above mentioned Deed of sale No. 6017 dated 30.04.1969, 1677 dated 25.02.1976, 3957 dated 25.07.1977 and 2796 dated 04.06.1977 have not been referred to us. Bank should obtain the above Deeds of Sale.

Subsequently, Mrs. Hasnin Kibria Sonia, Mr. Wahabuzzaman and 19 others  amicably partitioned  the land  amongst them vide Deed of Partition No. 6196 dated 04.06.1996.

Union Knitting and Dyeing has become owner land measuring 42.24 decimals equivalent to 4224 ajutangsha by way of purchase from Mrs. Hasnin Kibria Sonia and Mr. Wahabuzzaman vide Deed of Sale No. 7318 dated 11.04.2005 and 18026 dated 25.07.2005 respectively.
