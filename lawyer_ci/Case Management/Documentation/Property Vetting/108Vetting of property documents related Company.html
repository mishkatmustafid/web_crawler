8. Chain of Ownership :

Upon perusal of C.S. Khatian No.40, it appears that land measuring 87 decimal under Dag No. 302 along with other lands in several Dags were recorded in the name of Karim Baksh.

Karim Baksh sold the land to Paia Gazi vide Deed of Sale No. 2299 dated 29.02.1928. After the death of Paia Gazi his son Peskar Ali became owner of the land by way of succession. In the S.A. operation the land measuring 82 decimal under Dag No. 302 was recorded in the name of Peskar Ali. And in the R.S. operation land measuring 82 decimal under Dag No. 547 was recorded in his name as well.

Thereafter, Peskar Ali sold land measuring 19 decimal and 17.50 decimal to Mosammat Mehzabin Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 3496 dated 16.04.19977 and Deed of Sale no. 8029 dated 17.03.1977 respectively and land measuring 19 dercimal and 17.50 decimal to Monwara Akmal Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 3495 dated 16.04.19977 and Deed of Sale No. 8029 dated 17.03.1977 respectively.

Mahibur Rahman Chowdhury became owner of the land measuring 36.50 decimal by way of purchase from Monwara Akmal Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 497 dated 04.02.1987 and land measuring 36.50 decimal from minor Khan Mehzabin Khan through her mother Monwara Akmal Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 498 dated 04.02.1987. We have not been provided with any documents showing that Monwara Akmal Khan was permitted by the Court to sell her minor daughter’s land. Please note that in the Deed of Sale Nos. 497, 498 dated both 04.02.1987 S.A. Khatian number was mentioned as 120 and Hal Khatian as 154, but we found that final S.A. Khatian number is 102.

Then, Mahibur Rahman Chowdhury died leaving behind only wife Mosammat Roushan Ara Chowdhury, daughter Safia Rahman Chowdhury, Afia Rahman Chowdhury,  Halima Rahman Chowdhury, Fatema Rahman Chowdhury, son Yasir-bin-Rahman Chowdhury as his legal heirs for the land.

The present owner Company 1 has become owner of the land measuring 73 decimal by way of purchase from Mosammat Roushan Ara Chowdhury, minor Safia Rahman Chowdhury, minor Afia Rahman Chowdhury, minor Halima Rahman Chowdhury, minor Fatema Rahman Chowdhury, minor Yasir-bin-Rahman Chowdhury vide Deed of Sale No. 21353 dated 04.11.2002. The said minors were represented in the Deed of Sale No. 21353 dated 04.11.2002 by their mother Mosammat Roushan Ara Chowdhury by receiving permission from learned 2nd Assistant Judge and Family Court, Dhaka in the Order passed in the Suit No. 54/2000. But we have not been provided with the certified copy of the Order through which Mosammat Roushan Ara Chowdhury receipt permission to sell her childrens’ land.
