Upon perusal of C.S. Khatian No.40, it appears that land measuring 87 decimal under Dag No. 302 along with other lands in several Dags were recorded in the name of Karim Baksh.

Karim Baksh and Sheikh Monuruddin sold the said land to Paia Gazi vide Deed of Sale No. 2299 dated 29.02.1928. After the death of Paia Gazi his son Peskar Ali became owner of the land by way of succession. In the S.A. operation the land measuring 82 decimal under Dag No. 302 was recorded in the name of Peskar Ali. And in the R.S. operation land measuring 82 decimal under Dag No. 547 was also recorded in his name.

Thereafter, Peskar Ali sold:

  • land measuring 19 and 17.50 decimal to Mosammat Mehzabin Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 3496 dated 16.04.19977 and 8029 dated 17.03.1977
  • land measuring 19 and 17.50 decimal to Monwara Akmal Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 3495 dated 16.04.19977 and 8029 dated 17.03.1977 respectively.

  • the land measuring 36.50 decimal by way of purchase from Monwara Akmal Khan vide Deed of Sale No. 497 dated 04.02.1987 and
