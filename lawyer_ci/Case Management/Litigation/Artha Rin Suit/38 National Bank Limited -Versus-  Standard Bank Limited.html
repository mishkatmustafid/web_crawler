SHEWETH

01. That the plaintiff bank has filed Money Suit No. 38 of 2003 in Joint District Judge 5th Court, Dhaka on 08.07.2003 against the defendant bank as principal defendant. Subsequently the said suit has been transferred from 5th Joint District Judge, Dhaka to 3rd Artha Rin Court, Dhaka and renumbered as Artha Rin Suit No. 247 of 2003.

04. That this application has been filed bonafide.
