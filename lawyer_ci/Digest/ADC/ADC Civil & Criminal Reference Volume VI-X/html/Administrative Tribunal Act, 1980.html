We would like to give more explicit reply to the questions raised by the learned Advocate-on-Record for the pe­titioner. It is important to note that amended proviso 2 was added beforethe original 2nd proviso and as a result, the 2nd proviso was relegated to the 3rd proviso. The 1st proviso, in essence, provides that where there is a higher ad­ministrative authority having the power to set aside, vary or modify an order, de­cision or action to be challenged before the Administrative Tribunal, the Admin­istrative Tribunal shall not entertain a case until such higher authority takes a decision on the matter.
