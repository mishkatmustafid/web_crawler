Nari o Shisu Nirjatan Daman Ain 2000, Section 11KA

Since, the trial of the case could not be concluded within reasonable time after it was sent back for fresh trial under proper law the accused-petitioner prayed for bail before the High Court Division and by the impugned judg­ment and order the High Court Divi­sion rejected the said prayer making observation that since there was defi­nite direction from the High Court Di­vision that the accused shall remain in custody till conclusion of the trial, the court had no jurisdiction to entertain his prayer for bail. Lokman Sarder vs. The State (Nazmun Ara Sultana J) (Criminal) 9 ADC 719

The prosecution examined several wit­nesses before the tribunal to prove the charge against the accused persons. The accused persons were also exam­ined under section 342 of the Code of Criminal Procedure and both of them pleaded not guilty. The tribunal, on consideration of the evidence and other facts and circumstances, found the present accused-respondent guilty under section 9(1) of the Nari-O-Shishu Nirjatan Daman Ain, 2000 and convicted him thereunder and sen­tenced him to suffer imprisonment for life along with fine. The tribunal ac­quitted the other accused of the charge levelled against him. The State vs. Md. Kamal Hossain (Nazmun Ara Sultana J) (Criminal) 9 ADC 709

Accused Chaklet, Mostak, Masud ob­structed them and forcibly kidnapped Jannatul Ferdous @ Sagorika by a red coloured microbus. Informant tried to resist them when accused Mostak in­flicted dagger blow causing injury to the left hand of informant and accused Moniruzzaman @ Chaklet pointed a pistol and thereafter, the accused per­sons hurriedly left the place of occur­rence with victim Sagorika by microbus. Md. Moniruzzaman alias Chaklet vs. The State (Mohammad Ea-zlul Karim J) (Criminal) 8 ADC 711
