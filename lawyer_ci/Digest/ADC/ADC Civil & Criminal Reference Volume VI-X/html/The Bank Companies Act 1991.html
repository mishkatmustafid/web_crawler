Section 27 KaKa

 

Section 27 ka ka of Bank Companies Act, 1991 provides for identification and preparation of a list of defaulter ba­ilees by the bank itself and then to send it to Bangladesh Bank. The purpose of sending such list to the Bangladesh Bank having regulatory authority, is clearly stated in sub-sections (2) and (3) to the effect that Bangladesh Bank shall distribute such list to other banks and fi­nancial institutions which are prohibited from giving loan to the defaulter. Md. Abul Kashem vs. Mahnutdul Hasan (Syed Mahmud Hossain J) 489
