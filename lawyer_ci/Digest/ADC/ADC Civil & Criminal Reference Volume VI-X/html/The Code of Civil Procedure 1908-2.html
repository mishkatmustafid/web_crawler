Section 115

 

Where there is an error apparent on the face of the record, whether error oc­curred by reason of the counsel's mis­take or it crept in by reason of an oversight on the part of the Court, is not a circumstance which can affect the ex­ercise of jurisdiction of the Court to re­view its decision. Md. Abdur Rashid Akand vs. Md. Raisuddin (S.K. Sinha J)(Civil) 8 ADC 39
