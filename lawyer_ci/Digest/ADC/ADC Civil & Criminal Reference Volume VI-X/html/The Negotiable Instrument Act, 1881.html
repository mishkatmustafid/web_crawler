Section 138

We are of the view that the operation of section 138 of the Act, 1881 cannot be obstructed or, in any way, circum­vented by the mere fact of filing of a suit by the drawer of the dishonoured cheque in civil Court whatever allega­tions may be in the plaint about the same and the relief prayed for therein, because such a device shall totally make the section itself nugatory. How­ever, if a holder or the payee gets hold of a dishonoured cheque by fraudulent means or forgery, the drawer of the cheque shall have the liberty to take such defence during the trial .Md. Arif-Uz-Zaman. vs. The State (Md. Abdul Wahhab Miah J) (Criminal) 8 ADC 975
