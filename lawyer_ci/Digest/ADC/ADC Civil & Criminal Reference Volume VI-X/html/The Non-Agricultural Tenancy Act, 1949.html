The learned Judge of the High Court Division found that the only issue which was decided by the trial Court in Title Suit No. 5 of 1994, instituted by the pre-emptee was in respect of the validity of the deed of Heba bel Ewaj by which Rabeya Khatun transferred the land purchased by her in favour of her son, the pre-emptor. He further found that the question of co-sharership of the pre-emptor was not at all decided in the said suit, as such, it was rightly decided in the instant pre-emption case, al­though against the pre-emptor. On these findings, the learned Judge of the High Court Division held that the issue of co-sharership is not barred by the principle of resjudicata, waiver and ac­quiescence, as submitted on behalf of the pre-emptor. Muhammad Ali vs. Mrs. Khaleda Rahman (A.B.M. Khairul Hague J) (Civil) 7 ADC 568
