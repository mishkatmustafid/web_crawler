Refund of benefits

 

It is a settled law that even if an incum­bent after the termination of his service engages himself in any other service or business transaction, then any amount that he might earn by way of salary from such service or as profits of such busi­ness may be set off against his salary due on his reinstatement in the service. Bangladesh vs Rakibuddin Ahmed (Bimalendu Bikash Roy Choudhury J)(Civil) 2ADC 500
