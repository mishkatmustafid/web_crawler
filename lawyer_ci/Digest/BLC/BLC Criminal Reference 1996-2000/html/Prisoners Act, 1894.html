Prisoners Act [IX of 1894]


Section 30—

A condemned prisoner, in view of section 30 of the Prisoners Act, 1894 and the Rules 617, 979 to 981 and 1051 to 1058 of the Jail Code is not entitled to get Division-I.

Major (Retd) Bazlul Huda vs State 5 BLC 254.
