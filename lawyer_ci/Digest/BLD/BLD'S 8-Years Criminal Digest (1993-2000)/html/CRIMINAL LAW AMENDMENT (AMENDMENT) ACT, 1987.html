CRIMINAL LAW AMENDMENT (AMENDMENT) ACT, 1987

(XIII OF 1987)

 

Section—8(a)

Section 8(a) of the Criminal Law Amendment (Amendment) Act (XIII of 1987) very much applies to this case. The

proceedings of the instant case before the learned Divisional Special Judge having not been concluded within the statutory period of 2 years from the date of commencement of the said Act, further proceeding of the case are liable to be stopped and the accused petitioner released.

Sardar Abdul Hoque Vs. The State, 13BLD(HCD)274.
