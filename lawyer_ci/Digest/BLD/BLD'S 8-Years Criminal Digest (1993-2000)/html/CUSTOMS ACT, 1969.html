CUSTOMS ACT, 1969 (IV OF 1969)

 

Section—156(8)

Special Powers Act, 1974 (XIV of 1974)

Section—25B

Smuggling was made punishable under section 156(8) of the Customs Act but subsequently it has been made punishable under section 25B of the Special Powers Act, 1974.

Md. Shaft Vs. The State, 14BLD (HCD) 322
