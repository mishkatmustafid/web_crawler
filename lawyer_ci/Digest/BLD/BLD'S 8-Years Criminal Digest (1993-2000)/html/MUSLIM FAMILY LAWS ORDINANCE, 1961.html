MUSLIM FAMILY LAWS ORDINANCE, 1961 (VIII OF 1961)

 

Section—6(5)(b)

As the complaint has compromised the case with her husband, the parties should be permitted to compound the offence for enabling them to live peacefully. The accused-petitioner is acquitted of the charge brought against him on compromise.

Azizul Haque Howlader Vs The State and another, 14BLD(HCD)149
