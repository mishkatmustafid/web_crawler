AGRANI BANK (EMPLOYEES) SERVICE REGULATIONS, 1991


Agrani Bank (Employees) Service Regubiions, 1991—Regulation No. 28(II)(e)— Whether second show cause mandatory fore dismissal—Agrani Bank Ltd. Service Rules and Regulations not in existence when !epartmenta1 proceeding was initiated against the appellant—Appellant dismissed from service after coming into force of the Agrani Bank (Employees) Service Regulation, 1981 Service Rules come into effect on 12.9.81, departmental inquiry commenced on 12.1.82, ‘inquiry report submitted on 2.6.82 and dismissed from service on 29.11.82).

Appellant’s cases governed by the provision of Regulation No. 28(II)(e) and the appellant was entitled to second show cause notice before the order of dismissal was passed.

Faziul Kari,n Vs. Agrani Bank, 11 BLD (HCD) 528.
