ATTIA FOREST (PROTECTION) ORDINANCE (XXXII OF 1982)


Sections—3 and

Abatement of legal proceedings challenging validity of the notifications constituting reserved forest known as Attia Forest— There is no scope for challenging the validity of the Notification of 1 927 under the Forest Act (XVI of 1927) constituting reserved forest after the promulgation of the Attia Forest (Protection) )Ordinance (XXXIII of l98)— All judgments, decrees or orders in respect of Attia Forest shall have no force and all suits, appeals and other legal proceedings challenging constitution of Attia Reserved Forest shall abate—Independently of the Attia Forest (Protection) Ordinance, the Notification of 1927 constituting reserved forest known as Attia Forest was valid one— Forest Act (XIV of 1927)S. 20.

Bangladesh Vs. Abdul Baset Mia; 6 BLD (AD) 62.
