BANGLADESH (RESTORATION OF EVACUEE PROPERTY) ORDER (P.O. 13 OF 1972)

 

Articles—3, 7, 9 and 10

Administrative Law—Tribunal set up under a special law to hear appeals and fully adjudicate the dispute with power to take evidence—Tribunal cannot exercise power of remand unless the law grants such power.

Nani Gopal Saha Vs. Jatindra Lal Chowdhury and others; 1 BLD (AD) 294.
