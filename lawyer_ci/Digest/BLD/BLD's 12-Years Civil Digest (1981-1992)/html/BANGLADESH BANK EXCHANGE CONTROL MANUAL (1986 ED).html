Chapter XIV, Section 1, Paras I and 5(f) and (g)

Import under Wage Earner’s Scheme— Whether goods imported under this Scheme could be refused necessary clearance on the plea of violation of Import Policy of 1986— 87—When the goods being drugs and medicines are imported under the Wage Earners Scheme with the approval of the concerned authority arid the validity of L!C expires before releasing the goods but the period of LIC is extended and revalidated by the authority, there is no violation of any provision of law or rules and regulations of the Import Policy in as much as special relaxation has been provided by the Bangladesh Bank Exchange Control Manual of 1986 Edition with regard to import of goods by petitioner’s foreign exchange under the Wage Earner’s Scheme— Provision of the Import Policy in question has no manner of application in the present case as the import is not on cash basis—Import Policy Order 1986—87 Cl. 15(VII) & (VIII).

M/s. Green Pharmacy Vs. Government of Bangladesh, represented by its Secretary, Ministry of Health and Family Planning and others; 1O BLD (HD) 8O.
