BANGLADESH BIMAN EMPLOYEES (SENIORITY OF FREEDOM FIGHTERS) RULES, 1980

 

Rules—2, 3 and 4

When the legislature in Parent legislation did not ‘signify any intention to classify the employees of Biman into freedom fighters and non-freedom fighters, such classification could not be done by the Government in its Rule making power while exercising the delegated authority under the parent, legislation, since that would amount to acting beyond the purposes of the legislation itself—Rule conferring two years ante dated seniority to freedom fighter employees of Biman is ultra vires rule making power under the Ordinance— Bangladesh Biman Corporation Ordinance (XIX of 1977), sections. 7, 14 and 31(2)(d).

Aminul Islam and ors. Vs. Bangladesh Biman Corporation and ors. 2 BLD (HCD) 1.

 

Rule—4

Power to alter terms and conditions of service does not and cannot imply the power to make fresh classification of employees which was not contemplated in the parent legislation—Bangladesh B iman Corporation Ordinance (XIX of 1977), Section.32 (2)(d).

Aminul Islam and ors. Vs. Bangladesh Biman Corporation and ors. 2 BLD (HCD) 1.

 

Rule—4

Fundamental right tç equality—Classification of freedom fighters and non-freedom fighters is unreasonable and conferment of two years ante dated seniority to freedom fighters is grossly discriminatory and violative of Article 27 of the Constitution- Constitution of Bangladesh, 1972, Article 27.

Aminul Islam and ors. Vs. Bangladesh Biman corporation and ors. 2 BLD (HCD) 1.

Bangladesh Biman Employees (Seniority of Freedom Fighters) Rules, 1980 remedied an anomalous situation—classification of freedom fighter employees and non—freedom fighter employees is reasonable—Rules have laid down reasonable guidelines for selection or identification of freedom fighters so that certain benefits may be extended uniformly, without picking and choosing, to all employees of the Corporation who come within rule 3—Rule 4 of Bangladesh Biman Employees Seniority of Freedom Fighters) Rules not violative of Article 27 of the Constitution.

Aminul Islam and others Vs. Bangladesh Biman Corporation and other. 2 BLD (HCD) 1.
