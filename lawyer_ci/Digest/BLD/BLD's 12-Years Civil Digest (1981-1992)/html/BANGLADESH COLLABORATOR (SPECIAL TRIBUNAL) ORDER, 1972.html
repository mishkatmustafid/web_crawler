BANGLADESH COLLABORATOR (SPECIAL TRIBUNAL) ORDER (P.O. 8 OF 1972)

 

Article—2(b)(v) read with part IV(b) to the schedule of P.O. No. 8 of 1972

Guilty mind in criminal trial—Trial of Collaborator—Guilty mind or guilty knowledge—Evidence of P.Ws. and facts and circumstances of the case do not show any guilty knowledge or guilty mind of the accused in associating himself with the District Peace Committee.

Saad Ahmed Vs. The State; 3 BLD (HCD) 75

Ref. 26 DLR 183.

 

Article—2(b) (iii)

Waging War—It means waging war in the manner usual in a war—It does not mean using highly emotional opinionated language against the Government in public speeches— Penal Code (XLV of 1860) S. 121.

Majibur Rahman Vs. The State; 3 BLD (HCD) 158.
