BANGLADESH COMMITTEE OF MANAGEMENT (TEMPORARY ARRANGEMENT) ORDER (P.O. 117 OF 1972)

 

Article—2(b) and 3

Administrative Law—Government’s action touching rights of citizens must be authorised by law—Mahila Samity coming into existence in February, 1972 does not come within the mischief of P.O. 117 of 1972—Government’s action dissolving committee of management of Mahila Samity and appointing ad hoc committee is void.

Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh Vs. Dr. Nilima Ibrahim; 1 BLD (AD) 296.

Ref. 32DLR 201 affirmed on different ground.
