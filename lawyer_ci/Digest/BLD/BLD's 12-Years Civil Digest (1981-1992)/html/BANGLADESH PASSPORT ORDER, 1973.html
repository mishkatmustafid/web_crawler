BANGLADESH PASSPORT ORDER (P.O. 9 OF 1973)

 

Articles—7 and 11

Passport—A person can be denied entry into Bangladesh if he does not possess a valid passport—Suppression of material facts in obtaining passport does not render the passport invalid—For such suppression the holder of passport may be otherwise dealt with but cannot be denied entry into Bangladesh— Passport Act (XXXIV of 1920) Ss. 3, 4 and 5—Passport (Offence) Act (LVI of 1952) S .3—Passport Rules, 1974, R.9—Passport Rules, 1955, Rs. 4 and 6.

Zamiruddin Ahmed Vs. Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh; 1 BLD (HCD) 304.
