BANGLADESH RIFLES ORDER, 1972 (P.O. No. 148 OF 1972)

 

Article—11

Discharge of B.D.R. personnel from service—When orders of discharge not sus-, tamable—The orders of discharge having been passed after allegedly instituting disciplinary proceedings and it being an admitted fact that the petitioners were all along unaware of such proceedings and the procedure provided in law having not been followed the impugned orders of discharge from service cannot be sustained—Bangladesh Rifles (Special provisions) Ordinance (LXXXV of 1976) Ss. 6—8.

Md. Faziur Rahman and others vs. Government of Bangladesh and others; 10 BLD (HCD) 65.
