BANGLALADESH ALLOCATION RULES, 1976

 

Rules framed under Fundamental Rules 45 may only lay down principles governing allotment of residence to officers and matters incidental to occupation thereof—Bangladesh Allocation Rules. 1976 so framed have not created any vested right in favour of the allotees, nor any claim of vested right could arise out of such rules—Authority may at any me terminate allotment without assigning any reason.

Md. Shahidullah Mia Vs. Secretary, Ministry of Public Works and Urban Development and others; 1 BLD (HCD) 129.
