CINEMATOGRAPH ACT (II of 1918)

 

Section—2(b)

Cinematograph-meaning of — VideoCassettee player, whether falls within the expression ‘composite equipment’ used in section 2(b) of the Act.

A careful reading of section 2(b) of the Cinematograph Act does not show that it excludes Video-Cassettee player—A restricted meaning cannot be given to the expression “Cinematograph” used in the section— The word is comprehensive enough to include a Video-Cassette player as the expression “Cinematograph” means a composite equipment used for production, projection and exhibition of motion picture film—Therefore, Video—Cassettee player falls within the expression ‘composite equipment’ used in section 2(b) of the Act.

Md. Aktarul alias Md. Nipu Vs. The State; 11 BLD (HCD) 392.
