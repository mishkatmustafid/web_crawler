CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES IN DEFENCE SERVICE (CLASSIFICATION, CONTROL AND APPEAL) RULES, 1961

 

Rules —8 and 9

Service matter—Compulsory retirement is a major penalty and can only he imposed upon a full enquiry under the Rules—after reinstatement the petitioner being governed by the Civil Service Regulations. He cannot be made to retire normally only after 12 years of service.

Mostafa Izzat Ali Chowdhury Vs. Government of Bangladesh and others; 2 BLD (HCD) 109.
