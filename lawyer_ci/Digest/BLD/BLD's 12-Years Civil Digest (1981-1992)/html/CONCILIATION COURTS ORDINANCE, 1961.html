CONCILIATION COURTS ORDINANCE, 1961 (XLIV OF 1961)

 

Section—3

Conciliation Courts Ordinance—The word ‘contrad’ has not been qualified in the Ordinance in any way and as such should embrace an oral contract as well as a written one.

Suchitra Kumar Das Vs. Sree Indu Bhushan Sarker; 1BLD (AD) 239

Ref: 21 DLR (SC) 39; 17 DLR 261; 17DLR 415.
