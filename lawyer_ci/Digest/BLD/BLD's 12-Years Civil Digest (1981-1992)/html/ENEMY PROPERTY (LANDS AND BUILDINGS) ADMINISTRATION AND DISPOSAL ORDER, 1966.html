ENEMY PROPERTY (LANDS AND BUILDINGS) ADMINISTRATION AND DISPOSAL ORDER, 1966

 

Section–4(3)(4)

 

Release—When the Custodian. Enemy Property released the property on the ground that the same is not Enemy Property he could not direct the tenant to handover the property to a person whose title is denied by the owner.

Mst. Aflabunessa Vs. Md. Shamsul I-faq Taiukder, 4BLI)(AD)273
