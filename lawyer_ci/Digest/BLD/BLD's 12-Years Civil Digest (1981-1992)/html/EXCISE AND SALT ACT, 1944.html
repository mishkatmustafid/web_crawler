EXCISE AND SALT ACT, 1944

 

Interpretation—The Excise -and Salt Act, 1944 like any other fiscal statute imposing a burden on the subject, is to be strictLy construed—Where there is any doubt, an interpretation favourable to the subject should he preferred.

The National Board of Revenue, Govt of Bangladesh and another Vs. Bata Shoe Company (Bangladesh) Ltd. 1IBLD (AD)1 12.
