GUARDIANS AND WARDS ACT, 1890

(VIII of 1890)

 

Sections—7 and 25

Father—Whether a father is to file an application under section 7 when he has already filed an application under section 25 of the Act—A father need not file an application under section 7 for his appointment as a guardian of the minor as he is the natural guardian of the minor child—The Court has authority to pass an order regarding custody when he has already filed an application under section 25 of the Act.

Mrs. Nilufar Majid Vs. Mokbul Ahuzed, 4BLD(HCD)79.

 

Section—17

Whether on remarriage a mother to tally loses her right of custody (hijanat)Z.A mother marrying a stranger loses her preferential right of custody over a minor child but that does not totally exclude her from such right if she is found otherwise fit by the Court.

Mrs. Nilufar Majid Vs. Mokbul A limed, 4BLD(HD) 79

Ref: 22 DLR 245—Cited.

Right of custody (hijanat) of the minor child—Under Muslim Law mother’s right to hijanat continues even after her divorce by the father of the minor till she takes a second husband when the right of custody belongs to father—Court has power to appoint her even after remarriage with a stranger although she loses her right of hijanat for the welfare of the minor.

Mrs. Nilufar Majid Vs. Mokbul Ahrned, 4BLD(HcD) 79

Ref: PLD 1955(Lahore)412; A.1.R. 1942 (Cal) 281: [LR 1942 (Kar) 363: AIR. 1936 (Pesh) 63: PLD 1963 (Karachi) 465—Cited.

 

Section—17

Question of Title—Whether question of title can be decided in a guardianship proceeding--Question of title to a property can not be decided in a proceeding under the Guardians and Wards Act.

Mst. Hematunnesa and others Vs. Abdul Aziz Sawdagar and others, 5BLD(HCD)264

 

Section—25

Guardianship of minor son—Principle of Islamic Law as to who is entitled to his custody—If the circumstances existed which justified the deprivation of a party of the custody of his child to whose custody lie was entitled under Muslim Law, Courts did not hesitate to do so—Courts power to determine the entitlement of a party to the hizanat is not limited to the mere observance of the age rule—The concept of the welfare of the minor child, whether below or above the age limit, sems to have been of paramount importance—Court rightly appointed the mother in preference to the father guardian of the minor boy for the welfare of the boy.

Md. Abdu Baker Siddique Vs. S.M.A. Bakar and others, 6BLD (AD)245
