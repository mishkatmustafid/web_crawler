HINDU WOMEN’S RIGHT TO PROPERTY ACT, 1937 (XVIII OF 1937)

 

Section—3

Hindu widow—Right to claim preemption—During her life time a Hindu widow is a complete owner and co-sharer of any property or holding of her deceased husband with the right to claim partition and consequently having the right to claim preemption—State Acquisition and Tenancy Act, 1950 (XXVIII of 1951), S. 96.

Rai Kishori Saha Vs. Md. Motaleb Au and others, 3BLD(HCD)1
