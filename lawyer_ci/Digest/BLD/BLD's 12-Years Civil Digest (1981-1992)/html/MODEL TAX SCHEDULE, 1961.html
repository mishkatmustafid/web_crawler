MODEL TAX SCHEDULE, 1961

 

Rules—1, 13 and 14

Power of Municipality to levy taxes and rates—Municipal Committee may levy all or any taxes or rates. If tax included rate, separate methods of levying taxes and rates would not have been prescribed.

Dacca Municipal Corporation Vs. Sonali Bank and others, 1 BLD (AD) 470
