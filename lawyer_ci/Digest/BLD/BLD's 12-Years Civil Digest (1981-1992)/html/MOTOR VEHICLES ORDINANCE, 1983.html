MOTOR VEHICLES ORDINANCE, 1983

 

Sections — 2(XXVI) and 8(3)

Drivers right to form Trade Union— Carrying of goods by trucks is a business as owners in course of their carrying the business either earn profits or incur loss. The drivers of the trucks are engaged by owners of the trucks and as workers they have right to form trade union for the purpose of regulating the relations between themselves and employers and for imposing restrictive condition on the conduct of the trade union or business:

Pabna Motor Employees Association Vs. The Member, Labour Appellate Tribunal and others, l1BLD (HDC) 451

 

‘The members of the Pabna Zilla Truck Chalak Union, citizens of Bangladesh, have the right to form the trade union and are entitled to have the registration of their trade union under section 8 of the Motor Vehicles Ordinance, 1983.

Pabna Motor Employees Association Vs. The Member, Labour Appellate Tribunal and others, 11BLD (HCD) 451.
