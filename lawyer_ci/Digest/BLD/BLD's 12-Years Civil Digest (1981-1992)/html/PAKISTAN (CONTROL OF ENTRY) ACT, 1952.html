PAKISTAN (CONTROL OF ENTRY) ACT, 1952

 

Section—4

Citizenship—When it is lost—Even if accused as a minor left with his mother for India and his mother had a migration certificate, that fact alone will not prove that he lost his citizenship of the then Pakistan—Prosecution has to prove that he acquired Indian citizenship.

Government of Bangladesh Vs. Advaita chandra Adilikary, 2BLD(HCD)197
