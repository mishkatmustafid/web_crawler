PORT RULES, 1966

 

Rule—80

Appointment of stevedores and handling contractors of inland ports—B IWTA as conservator of such ports can appoint stevedores and handling contractors and grant licence to them—Holding of public auction for such appointment is not prohibited.

The whole object of Rule 80 is to enable the Conservator of Ports to appoint stevedores and handling contractors and to regulate their activity within the port area by appropriate terms and conditions. The method of appointment by advertisement in the daily newspapers to hold public auction for selecting the highest bidder at such auction for appointment has been laid down in circulars and these are provided in the circulars in detail. Reading the provisions Of Rule 80 as a whole, we nowhere find the prohibition to hold public auction for the purpose of selecting stevedores and handling contractors.

Bangladesh Inland Water Transport Authority Vs. Bangladesh 1. W. T.A. Ghat Sramik Union and another, 2BLD (AD) 83.
