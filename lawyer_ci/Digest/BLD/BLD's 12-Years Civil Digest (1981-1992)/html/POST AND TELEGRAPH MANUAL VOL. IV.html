POST AND TELEGRAPH MANUAL VOL. IV

 

Article—31 1

Termination of Employee—If the Government has by contract, express or implied, or under the rules, the right to terminate the employment at any time, then such termination in the manner provided by the contract or the rules is prima facie and per se, not a punishment and as such it does not attract the provisions of Article 311 of the Post and Telegraphs Manual, Volume IV.
