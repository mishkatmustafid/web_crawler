POURASHAVA ELECTION RULES

 

Rule—73

Pourashava election—Election Commissions power to direct re-poll in Pourashava election—Election Commission has power to direct re-poll in appropriate circumstances and such power has been conferred by law for a free and fair election —Paurashava Election Rule is in identical term of the Union Parishad Election Rule—Controversial points which were disputed questions of fact involving Union Parishad Election Rule could be decided. It had been held in earlier judgments, on evidence by the Election Tribunal—The High Court Division has therefore erred in setting aside the order of re-poll by the Election Commission.

Golam Murshed Vs. Mostafizur Rahman and others, 10BLD (AD) 21
