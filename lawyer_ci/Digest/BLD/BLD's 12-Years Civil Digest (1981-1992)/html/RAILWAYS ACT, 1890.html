RAILWAYS ACT, 1890 (IX OF 1908)

 

Section—77

Limitation—Limitation in a suit for compensation against Railway for loss of goods—Notice must be given within the period of limitation.

Since there is no distinction of any loss caused by non-delivery and loss caused by negligence of the Railway as a carrier, a notice under section 77 of the Act must be given in all cases of claim against Railway for compensation within the period of limitation.

Chairman, Bangladesh Railway Board vs. Maintaz Hossain and others, 3 BLD (HCD) 143
