RETIREMENT RULES, 1968

 

Service matter

Retirement—MalafIde—Retirement of petitioners in violation of and without having regard to the provisions of Rules 3 of the Retirement Rules, 1968 is malafide, The ground

of redundancy on which the petitioners have been retired, is not the real ground for their retirement. it is a colourable exercise of power vested in the Chairman of the Pourashava.

Kanakiata Halder and others Vs. Barisal Pourashava and another, 10BLD (HCD) 381.
