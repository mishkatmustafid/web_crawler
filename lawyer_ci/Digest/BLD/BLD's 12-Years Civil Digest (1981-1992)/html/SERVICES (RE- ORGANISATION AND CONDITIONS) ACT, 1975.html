SERVICES (RE- ORGANISATION AND CONDITIONS) ACT, 1975

(XXXII of 1975)

 

Section —2 (b)

 

‘Pay’ — Definition of — Whether the self-same definition can be made applicable to office memorandums introducing Annual Festival Bonus, Dearness Allowance and Festival Allowance — Such Bonus and Allowances were no examples of statutory exercise of power — The memorandums were not published in the Bangladesh Gazette and not passed in exercise of powers under any provision of Act XXXII of 1975 wherein pay’ has been defined — These are executive orders for ex-gratia payments not in exercise of statutory power but in exercise of inherent discretionary power to grant financial relief — The definition of the word pay’ in the said Act cannot be imported in the office memorandums.

Md. Israil and others Vs. Secretary, Ministry of Industries and others, 9BLD (HCD)136.
