SONALI BANK STAFF SERVICE RULES

 

Rules — 40, 42(ii)

Departmental proceedings—Whether enquiry done by an officer not appointed by the authority having the power to dismiss the employee vitiates proceeding — The report of the, Enquiry Officer was forwarded to the Managing Directors and the Central Board of Director of the Bank after examining the report dismissed the plaintiff — Findings of the District Judge that the departmental proceeding and appointment of Enquiry Officer were not made by the Central Board of directors and his judgment and decree based thereon cannot be maintained—Bangladesh Bank (Na-aonalisation) Order. 1972 (P.O. 26 of 1972), Art. 10.

General Manager, Sonali Bank and ors. Vs. Md. Nurul Hoque Khan, 8BLD HCD)403
