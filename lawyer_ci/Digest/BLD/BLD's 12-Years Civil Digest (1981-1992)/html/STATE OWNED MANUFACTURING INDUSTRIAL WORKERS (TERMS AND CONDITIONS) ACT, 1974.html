STATE OWNED MANUFACTURING INDUSTRIAL WORKERS (TERMS AND CONDITIONS) ACT, 1974

(X OF 1974)

 

Section—3

Agreement with workers—The validity of—State-owned Manufacturing Industrial Workers (Terms and Conditions) Act. 1 974 and its subsequent amendment fixed the wages for spool packers—An earlier agreement with them fixing their wages has then lost its force—Labour Court’s order directing payment of wages under the agreement is without lawful authority.

M/s. carpeting Jute Mills Ltd. Vs. Chairman, Khu Inn Labour Court and others, 2 BLD (HCD)141.
