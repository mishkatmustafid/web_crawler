SUIT VALUATION ACT, 1887

(VII OF 1887)

 

Section—11

Objection as to jurisdiction of Court— Whether it is to be taken at the earliest opportunity—Objection as to jurisdiction due to overvaluation or undervaluation of suit or appeal should be taken at the earliest opportunity—This principle will not apply where there is no error in valuation.

Naresh Chandra Shaha and others Vs. Karizannessa and others, 6 BLD (HCD) 361.
