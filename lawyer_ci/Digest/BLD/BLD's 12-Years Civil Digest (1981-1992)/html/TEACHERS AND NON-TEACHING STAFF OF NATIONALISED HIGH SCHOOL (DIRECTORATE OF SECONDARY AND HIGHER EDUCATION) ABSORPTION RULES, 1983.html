TEACHERS AND NON-TEACHING STAFF OF NATIONALISED HIGH SCHOOL (DIRECTORATE OF SECONDARY AND HIGHER EDUCATION) ABSORPTION RULES, 1983

 

Rule—2(h)

Absorbtion—The decision to absorb any of those teachers of the school who were appointed before the date of declaration to nationalise the school is rational, reasonable and within Government authority. It cannot be said to be arbitrary, whimsical and the Government in these circumstances cannot be compelled to appoint the petitioner or any other subsequently appointed teachers of the nationalised school.

Nikhil Kumar Sarker Vs. Bangladesh and others, 11BLD (HCD) 402.
