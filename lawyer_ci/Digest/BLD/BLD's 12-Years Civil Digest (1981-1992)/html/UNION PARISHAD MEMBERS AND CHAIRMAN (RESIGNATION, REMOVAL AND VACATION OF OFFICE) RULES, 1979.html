UNION PARISHAD MEMBERS AND CHAIRMAN (RESIGNATION, REMOVAL AND VACATION OF OFFICE) RULES, 1979

 

Rules—8, 10 and 17

Local Government—Removal of Chairman—Ordinance providing for removal of Chairman by resolution of two—thirds of the total number of elected members—Rules including the Chairman in the number of elected members for the resolution—Rules 8 and 10 are ultra vires to the extent they provide for the inclusion of the Chairman’s vote in the calculation df the required two-third of the total number of elected members for passing the resolution—Local Government Ordinance, 1976 (XC of 1976), Sections. 5 and 13.

Azizul Molla Vs. Taher Au and others, 1 BLD (HCD) 325.
