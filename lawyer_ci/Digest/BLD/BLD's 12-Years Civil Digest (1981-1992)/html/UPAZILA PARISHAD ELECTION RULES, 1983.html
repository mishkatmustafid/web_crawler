UPAZILA PARISHAD ELECTION RULES, 1983

 

Power of Election Commission—The Election Commission has got power and jurisdiction to organise, hold and conduct election and it is also its duty to see that election is held i fair and honest manner. But this power is to be exercised subject to the election Rules.

Abdur Rouf Mia Vs. Faziur Rahman and others, 11BLD (AD) 62

 

Rule—27

Power of Election Commission— Where there are contradictory reports and materials before the Election Commission about adournments of the poll and submission of result by the Presiding Officer, the Election Commission has the power either to direct repoll or to direct acceptance of the result. This power has been vested in the Election Commission under Rule 27 and there is no question of exercise of the planery power by the Election Commission in such a situation.  

Scum Ullah Bahadur Vs. The Election Commission and others, 11 BLD (HCD) 548.
