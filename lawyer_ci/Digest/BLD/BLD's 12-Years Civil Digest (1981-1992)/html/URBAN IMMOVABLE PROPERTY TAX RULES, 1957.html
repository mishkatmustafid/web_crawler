URBAN IMMOVABLE PROPERTY TAX RULES, 1957

 

Rule—10

Urban Immovable Property Tax—Rule 10 gives power to amend or alter the valuation only in cases where assessment is yet to be completed and tax is payable—It does not give power to re-open a closed assessment.

Assessing Officer, Narayanganj Range and others Vs. Burmah Eastern Limited, 1 BLD (AD) 450.
