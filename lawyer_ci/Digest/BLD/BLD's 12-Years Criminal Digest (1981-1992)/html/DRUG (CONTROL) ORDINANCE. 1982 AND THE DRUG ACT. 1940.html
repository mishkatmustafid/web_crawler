Offence under the Drug Ordinance _Forum for investigation and trial — Under the Drug (Control) Ordinance, 1982 special procedure has been provided for investigation of the offence under the Ordinance by a designated class of officers and Special Court has been set up for the purpose — An ordinary Magistrate has no power to try such a case — A case of this nature can only be tried by the Drug Court situated at Dhaka_The case was accordingly transferred to the Drug Court at Dhaka for trial — Drug (Control) Ordinance (VIII of 1982) S 22 — The Drug Act (XXIII of 1940) S 22 — Code of Criminal Procedure (V of 1898) Ss 155 and 525 A Kalipada Shaha Vs.
