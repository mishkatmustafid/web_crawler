Anti-Corruption Act 1957 (Xxvi Of 1957).

 

Section—3(2)

Anti-Corruption Manual Articles—31 and 50

When the Court is in seisin of a matter involving a question of right, title and interest relating to any immovable property, the Bureau of Anti-corruption has no jurisdiction or authority to hold any inquiry under Articles 31 and 50 of Anti-Corruption Manual, either suo motu or upon an application of any person—the Anti-Corruption may, however, inquire in to a specific case under an order of the court, otherwise not.

Humayun Majid v. Bangladesh Bureau of Anti-corruption, 21 BLD (HCD) 551

Ref: Durga Das v. Emperor AIR 1943 Lah. 28.
