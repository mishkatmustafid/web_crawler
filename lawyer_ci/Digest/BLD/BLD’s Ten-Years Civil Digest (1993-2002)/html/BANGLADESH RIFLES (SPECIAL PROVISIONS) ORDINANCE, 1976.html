BANGLADESH RIFLES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ORDINANCE (LXXXV OF 1976)

 

Sections—6 to 8

Discharge of B.D.R. personnel from service—The orders of discharge having been passed after allegedly instituting disciplinary proceedings and the procedure provided in law having not been followed the impugned orders of discharge from service cannot be sustained—Bangladesh Rifles Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 148 of 1972).

Md. Faziur Rahman and others Vs. Government of Bangladesh and others. 10 BLD (HCD) 65.
