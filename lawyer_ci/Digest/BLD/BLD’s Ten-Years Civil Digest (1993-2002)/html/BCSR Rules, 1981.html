BCSR Rules, 1981

 

Rule—3

Procedure for recruitment subject to the instructions relating to reservation for the purpose of clause (3) of Article 29 of the Constitution and appointment to a service shall be made in accordance with the provisions of schedule II.

Sk. Abdur Rashid and others Vs Bangladesh, 21 BLD (HCD) 127.
