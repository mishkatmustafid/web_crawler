Bangabanuhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University Act 1998 (Act I Of 1998)

 

Sections—6 and 45

The Post Graduate Cell of the Academic Council of the Dhaka Medical College has no authority to change Government and University policy. The Cell is no legal entity. The Academic Council has the power to make recommendations only and not to make any decision itself.

Dr. Manzoor Rasheed Chowdhury v. The Principal, Dhaka Medical College, Dhaka and others, 22BLD (HCD) 6

Ref: Ritesh R. Sha v. Dr. Y.H. Yamul and Others AIR 1996 SC 1378; Dr. Vinay Eampal v. State of Jammu and Kashmir and others AIR 1983 SC.
