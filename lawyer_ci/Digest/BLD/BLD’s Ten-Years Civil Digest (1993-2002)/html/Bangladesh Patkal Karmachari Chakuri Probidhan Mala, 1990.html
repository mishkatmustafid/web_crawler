Bangladesh Patkal Karmachari Chakuri Probidhan Mala, 1990

 

Rule—43(8)

It provides that if the authority concerned fails to take final decision against a person within 180 working days when the charge has been framed he will be considered to be exonerated from the charge automatically. But in the instant case the impugned order was passed after expiry of 720 days. Moreover, no j second show cause notice or inquiry report was served upon the petitioner in total disregard to the mandatory provision of law and as such the impugned dismissal order is illegal and without any lawful authority.

Mallick Afzal Hossain Vs Bangladesh Jute Mills Corporation and others, 18 BLD (HCD) 686.
