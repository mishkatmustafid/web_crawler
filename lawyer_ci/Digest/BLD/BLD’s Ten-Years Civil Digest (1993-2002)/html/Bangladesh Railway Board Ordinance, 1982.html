Bangladesh Railway Board Ordinance, 1982 (24 Of 1983)

 

Section—2(2)

By virtue of clause (e) of Section 2 of Ordinance No. 24 of 1983, respondent 2 has become government employee .and the proceeding  instituted by her in 1985 under Section 34 of Industrial Relations Ordinance was incompetent inasmuch as on amendment of the Bangladesh Railway Board Ordinance, 1976, employees of the Board became employees of the Government to whom Section 34 of the I.R.O is not available.

The General Manager, Bangladesh Railway (West) and others Vs. The Chairman Labour Court, Rajshahi and another, 13 BLD (HCD) 325.
