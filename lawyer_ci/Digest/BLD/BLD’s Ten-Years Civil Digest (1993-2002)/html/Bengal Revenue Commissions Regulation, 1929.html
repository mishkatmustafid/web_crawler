Bengal Revenue Commissions Regulation, 1929

 

Section—2(1)

Creation and naming of a new Division on the authority of Section 2(1) of the Bengal Revenue Commissions Regulation, 1929 does not offend any of the fundamental rights envisaged under Part-Ill of the Constitution of Bangladesh and as such the Gazette Notification published to that effect cannot be declared to have been made without lawful authority.

Mizan Howlader Vs. Bangladesh and another, 16 BLD(HCD)5
