Chittagong Development Authority Employees (Service) Regulation, 1990

 

Regulation—56(2)

The removal of the petitioner under Regulation 56(2) is not a termination simpliciter. As a matter of fact it is not a case of termination but it is a case of removal from service in the garb of termination.

Chittagong Development Authority and others Vs Engineer Abul Kalam and another, 19 BLD (AD) 9.
