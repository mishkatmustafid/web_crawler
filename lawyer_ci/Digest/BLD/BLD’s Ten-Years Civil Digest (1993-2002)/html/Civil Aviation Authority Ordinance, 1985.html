Civil Aviation Authority Ordinance, 1985 (xxxviii Of 1985)

 

Section—5

‘Public Office’ is created by law conferring upon an individual and vesting him with some sovereign functions of the Government to be exercised by him for the benefit of the public during the term and tenure of his office prescribed by law. There is nothing in Civil Aviation Authority Ordinance, 1985 prescribing tenure of office of the Board of Authority. These persons do not hold any public office vesting them with any portion of the sovereign functions of the Government.

Md. Abdur Rahman Vs. Group Captain (Retd) Shamim Hossain, 17 BLD (HCD) 532.
