Excise And Salt Act, 1944

(I OF 1944)

 

Section—4(3)

Capacity Tax (Cinema House) Rules, 1983, Rule—9(2)

Without the prior permission of the customs authority the cinema hall owner has no right to increase or decrease the number of seats of a cinema hail. The factum of signing of the Register by an Assistant Collector of Customs cannot override the provisions of the Customs Act and the relevant rules.

Md. Anwar Hossain Vs The Secretary, Ministry of Finance, (Internal Resources Division) and others, 17 BLD (HCD) 230.
