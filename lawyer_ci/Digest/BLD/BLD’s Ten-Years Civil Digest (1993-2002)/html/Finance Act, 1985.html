Finance Act, 1985

 

In this Act an industrial company has been defined as a Company which is mainly engaged in the manufacturing process of goods. Tea to be offered for sale for its consumption at home or abroad passes through a manufacturing process. Therefore, a Tea Garden producing tea for sale comes within the definition of an industrial company although paddy may be grown in the Tea Garden.

Commissioner of Income Tax, Chittagong (South), Chittagong Vs. The Deandi Tea Company lid, 15 BLD (HCD) 372.

 

Finance Act, 1999

 

By the Finance Act 1999, income from a charity has been made taxable. This indicates that a charitable society can invest its money and profit earned from that investment will be taxable. (Per Mahmudul Amin Choudhury, CJ)

BRAC v. Professor Mozaffar Ahmed and others, 22 BLD (AD) 41.
