Forest Act, 1927

(XVI OF 1927)

 

Sections—4 and 20

In the Forest Act, 1927 there are provisions for filing objections against declaration of a reserve forest and after enquiry the Government is the final authority to constitute a forest. The plaintiffs having not challenged the notification under section 4 of the Forest Act and having failed to prove their claim of. settlement, it does not lie in their mouth to complain that there was no final notification under section 20 of the said Act.

Md. Nurul Islam and others Vs Bangladesh and others, 17 BLD(AD)91
