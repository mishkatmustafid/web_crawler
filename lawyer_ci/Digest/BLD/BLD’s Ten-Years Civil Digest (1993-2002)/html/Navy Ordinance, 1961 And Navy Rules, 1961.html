Navy Ordinance, 1961 And Navy Rules, 1961

 

Rule—14(5)

Rule 14(5) of the Navy Rules, empowers the Government to retire any officer at any time without assigning any reason whatsoever.

This Rule clearly excludes the application of the principle of audi alteram partem and it does not suffer from any legal or Constitutional infirmity.

Rear Admiral A.A. Mustafa Vs. Bangladesh, represented by the Secretary, Ministry of Defence, Old High Court Building, Dhaka, 14 BLD (AD) 16.
