Non-Cadre Class I And II Gazetted Officers (Customs, Excise And Vat) Appointment And Service Terms And Conditions Act, 2000

 

Section—8

By section 8 of Act XX of 2000 the services of Appraisers, Preventive Officers and Inspectors, who are of separate grades though their scales of pay may be the same, have been made inter-transferable without amalgamation of their services and rules framed accordingly. Accordingly, section 8 of Act XX of 2000 is found to be violative of articles 133 and 136 of the Constitution.

Mohammad Salahuddin Talukder v The Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh, 22 BLD (HCD) 335.
