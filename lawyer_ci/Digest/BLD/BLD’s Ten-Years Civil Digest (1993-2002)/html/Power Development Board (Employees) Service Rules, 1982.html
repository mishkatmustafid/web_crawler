Power Development Board (Employees) Service Rules, 1982

 

Rule—143

Although in sub-rule (2) no authority has been mentioned to whom a written defence has to be submitted, the written defence has to be submitted to the enquiry officer appointed to held the enquiry.

Chairman, Power Development Board, Dhaka and others v. Md. Faziul Haque, 22 BLD (AD) 85.

 

PREMISES RENT CONTROL ORDINANCE, 1963 & BARIVARA NIONTRON ODDHADESH, 1991

Sections—2 (Uma) and 18(1) (2)

Under Section 2 (Uma) of the Premises Rent Control Ordinance, the person who continues in possession after the termination of the tenancy and pays rent to the landlord is a tenant. Rent Control legislation is a special law which gives a greater protection to a tenant against eviction so long as he pays rent to the full extent allowable by the Ordinance and performs the conditions of the tenancy.

Abdul Aziz Vs. Md. Abdul Majid, 14 BLD (AD) 147.
