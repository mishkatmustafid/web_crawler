Public Corporations (Management Co-Ordination) Ordinance, 1986

(XL VIII OF 1986)

 

Sections—2(e) and 14A

Petitioner is not a worker as per definition provided by the Ordinance in clause (3) of section 2 and as such he cannot get the benefit of retirement at the age of 60 years as has been provided in section 14A of the same Ordinance.

Abdus Salam Molla Vs Platinum Jubilee Jute Mills Ltd and others, 21 BLD (HCD) 151.
