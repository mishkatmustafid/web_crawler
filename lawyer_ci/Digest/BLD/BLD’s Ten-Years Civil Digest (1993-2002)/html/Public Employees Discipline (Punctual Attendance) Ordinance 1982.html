Public Employees Discipline (Punctual Attendance) Ordinance 1982

 

Section—8

Section 8 of Ordinance XXXIV of 1982 gives a right to the employee whose salary has been deducted under Section 4 or 5 or 6 or 7 of the said Ordinance to file a representation to the authority concerned for a revision of the order and it is incumbent on the authority concerned to dispose of the representation by passing an order in accordance with Section 8 of the Ordinance.

Kazi Enamul Haque Vs. The Director General, Directorate of Relief and Rehabilitation and others, 13 BLD (HCD) 576.
