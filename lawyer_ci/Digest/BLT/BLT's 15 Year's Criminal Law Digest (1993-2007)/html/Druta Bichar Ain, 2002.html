Section-9(2)

We have gone through the order passed by the Magistrate which has simply and plainly asked the investigation officer to add relevant section of the Druta Bichar Ain provided the allegations do attract any of the section of the aforesaid Ain and so doing we do not think the Magistrate has in nay way exceeded his jurisdiction having the effect of interference with the course of investigation, rather, he has left the matter to the discretion and judgment of the investigating officer.

Monajjel Hossain Khan Vs. The State 13 BLT (AD) 25.
