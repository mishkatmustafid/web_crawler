Citation: 14 BLT (HCD) 389.

Subject: Durnity Daman

Delivery Date: 1970-01-01

 

 

Sections - 17 and 32(1)

Whether a private persons has right to file a petition of complaint before the commission

After commencement of law and establishment of the Durnity Daman Commission under section 3(1) of the Au| as an independent as an independent natural body to fulfill the object of the law an aggrieved party has right to file petition of complaint before the commission and on receipt of the complaint the commission is empowered to enquire and investigate in to the offence and on the result of the investigation issue necessary sanction order in favour of the complainant to institute the case and absence of rule does not stand on the way of the commission in exercising the power to issue necessary sanction.

Md. Moinuddin Vs. Md. Salim Ahmed & Ors 14 BLT (HCD) 389.
