Extradiction Act, 1974

Citation: 6 BLT (HCD)-138

Subject: Extradiction

Delivery Date: 1970-01-01

 

 

 

Section-4

Whether in absence of any extradition treaty between Bangladesh and the union of India the Government can extradic Anup Chetia to India.

Held: Thus, reading Section 4 of the Extradition Act, 1974 and section 3(1) read with section 2(f) (ii) of the Special Powers Act, 1974 together we hold that the Government of Bangladesh has sufficient legal authority to extradite Anup Chetia to India in response to the right of India to demand extradition of Anup Chetia.

Saiful Islam Dilder Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh 6 BLT (HCD)-138
