Section-10 & 12

Bail—provision for speedy trial had been made in the Ain of 2000 and speedy trial is also a right of the accused person and in the event of delay in trial accused persons may legally justified in asking for bail and used person cannot be detained in jail for indefinite period.

Judicial Mind in respect of Judicial Proceedings

The court should be unduly sensitive or touchy about its dignity. A mere audible remark or conduct, not meant for the court, should not be taken as an interference of interruption in Court's work while sitting at any stage of judicial proceedings. A certain degree of tolerance is always expected of a judicial officer.

Md. Moshiur Rahman Vs. The State 8 BLT (HCD)-209.
