e Regulations, Bangladesh, 1943

 

Regulation-29 read with Code of Criminal procedure, 1898 Section-202

The Metropolitan Magistrate was absolutely wrong in holding that Police Regulation is supplementary to the Code of Criminal Procedure in as much as Police Regulation is not supplementary to the Code of Criminal procedure rather Police Regulations are subservient to the Code of Criminal Procedure with respect to all procedures embodied in the Code including the procedure of a Judicial Inquiry.

Moni Begum @ Moni Vs. M. Shamsur Rahman & Ors. 10 BLT (HCD)-70.
