Section-4

The provision of this section is attracted only when the attempt is successful and complete. An attempt to realize money in the form of donation or otherwise has not been made punishable offence under this special law- relied on 15 BLD- 93.

Syed & Ors Vs. The State 4 BLT (HCD)-169.
