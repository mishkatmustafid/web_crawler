House Rent Control Act, 1991

 

Section -27 read with Code of Civil Procedure, 1908; Order 7 Rule -ll(d)

 

The proceeding under House Rent Control Act, 1991 is a summary one. The application under the said Act can not be termed as a plaint. What a plaint should contain has been provided by Order VII Rule 11 of the Code. Order VII Rule 11 of the Code prescribed the circumstances when a plaint would be liable to be rejected. In the cause title of the application of the said case, I find provisions of Sections 13 and 28 and relief is prayed for under section 27 of House Rent Control Act, 1991 have been invoked. In such circumstance the application of the said case is not a plaint. The application of the petitioner made under Order VII Rule 11 of the Code of Civil Procedure for rejection of such application is wholly misconceived.

 

Md. Mainul Haque Bhuyan Vs Md. Akkas Miah 17 BLT (HCD) 508.
