Non-Agricultural Tenancy Act, 1949

 

Section-24

 

It is the uniform view that character/nature of  particular   land   is   to   be   determined keeping in view the purpose for which tenancy was created or that settlement was made or that purpose for which lease was taken. It is also the uniform view that the land within the municipal area is pre-emptable under section 24 of the Non-Agricultural Tenancy Act.

 

Abdul Khaleque Vs. Abdul Noor & Ors 17 BLT (AD) 75.

 

Section-24 read with State Acquisition and Tenancy Act, 1950; Section-96

 

Since the land sought to be pre-empted is admittedly in the rural area and that it having not been established that the settlement of the land sought to be preempted was taken for the purpose other than agricultural purpose we are of the view that the High Court Division was not in error in discharging the Rule which was obtained against the judgment of the appellate Court dismissing the Miscellaneous case filed under section 24 of the Non-Agricultural Tenancy Act upon arriving at the finding that the land sought to be pre-empted is agricultural land.
