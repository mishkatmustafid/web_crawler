President Order No.7 of 1972

 

Article-27

 

House Building Finance Corporation has its own provisions for realisation of loan under Article 27 of President Order 7 of 1972 and jurisdiction has been exclusively given to District Judge as such for realising its loans only the District Judge has jurisdiction, no other court, Artha Rin Adalat being a subordinate court to District Judge has no jurisdiction to entertain any claim of House Building Finance Corporation.

 

Dr. Md. Asadullah & Anr Vs Sonali Bank Ltd. & Ors 19 BLT (HCD) 148.

 

 

President Order 98 of 1972

 

Article-4

 

In the case of society, Government may relax the ceiling. In the case in our hand the plaintiff is a registered society as such the plaintiff is fallen within the category of Article 4 of P.O. 98 of 1972.

 

Debishahar Agriculture Ltd. Vs Govt. of Bangladesh 19 BLT (HCD) 31.
