Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Dhaka Regulation, 1977

Citation: 2 BLT (AD)-116.

Subject: Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education

Delivery Date: 1970-01-01

 

 

Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Dhaka (Managing Committee of the recognized Non-Government Secondary Schools) 

 

Regulation 4 (1) with Regulation 20(1)

Whether the Managing Committee of a school managed by the Civil Aviation Welfare Association should have been constituted in terms of Regulation 4(1) or Regulation 20(1)?

The school being run as a departmental school of the Civil Aviation Authority for long 40 years the Board was justified in directing formation of an ad-hoc committee for constituting a regular committee in terms of Regulation 20 (1) of the Board’s Regulation. [Para- 11]

Chairman Civil Aviation Authority Vs K. A. Rouf & Ors 2 BLT (AD)-116.
