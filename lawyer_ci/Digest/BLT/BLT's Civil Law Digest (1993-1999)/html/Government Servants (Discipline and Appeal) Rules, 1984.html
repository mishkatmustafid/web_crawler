Bangladesh Forest Industries Development Corporation, Rule-15 and Government Servants (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1984, Sub Rule-II of Rule-7

The Corporation having violated the mandatory provision for removing an employee from service — to remove the petitioner from service having exceeding 120 days as required by sub-rule-II of rule-7 of rules 1984, the petitioner stood discharged as the charges brought against him — the removal of the petition from service by order 18.6.85 was illegal and without lawful authority. The order of removal being illegal, reducing the rank of the petitioner by way of punishment was also illegal — Rule made absolute. [Paras-6 & 7]

Md. Mohafuzur Rahman Vs. B. F I. D. C 2 BLT (HCD)-39.
