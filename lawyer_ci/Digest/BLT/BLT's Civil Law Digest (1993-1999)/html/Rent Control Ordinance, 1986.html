Rent Control Ordinance, 1986

 

Rent ControlOrdinance, 1986

 

Section-19(2)

Under S. 19(2) of the Premises Rent Control Ordinance a tenant is permitted to depositrent in case of bonafide doubt or dispute as to the persons entitled to receivethe rent—There is no wrong in the order of the High Court Division allowing thetenants application for making deposit of rent. [Para-4]

Al-Haj Miijahan Vs. M/S. Golden Biscuit 1 BLT (AD)-42.
