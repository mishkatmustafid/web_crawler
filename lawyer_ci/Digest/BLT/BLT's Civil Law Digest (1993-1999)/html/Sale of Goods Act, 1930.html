Sale of Goods Act, 1930

 

Sale ofGoods Act, 1930

 

Section-61 (2)

Section 61(2) provides that the court may award interest at such rate as it thinks fit onthe amount of the price to the seller in a suit by him for  the amount of the price from the date of thetender of the goods or from the date of which the price is payable. [Para- 12]
