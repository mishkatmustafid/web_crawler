State Acquisition Rules, 1950

 

Rule-23 (4)

Authority of the Revenue Officer in modifying record of right — Under rule 23(4) the Revenue Officer is empowered to direct excision of fraudulent entry from the record-of-rights after final publication thereof after consulting the previous settlement records, preliminary rent-rolls, draft record-of-rights, tahasil copies of records and Register-II. [Para-11]
