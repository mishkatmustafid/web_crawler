The Minimum Wages Ordinance, 1961

 

Section -2 (8) (b)

In the instant case the Board recommended a fixed amount of Tk-40/- per month as “trsow tcn” to all workers of the cinema halls irrespective of their rank and status as part of the fringe benefit along with the house rent and medical allowances, which has been accepted by the appellant by the impugned Notification. Thus in the facts and circumstances of the case the said ‘niri 11’ cannot be treated as travelling allowance within the meaning of section 2 (8) (b) of the Ordinance. [Para-6]

Government of Peoples Republic of Bangladesh Vs. Hasan Movies Ltd. & Ors. 4 BLT (AD)-50.
