Section-28

The land inquestion is situated within Laxmipur sub-registry whether the BegumgonjSub-registrar had jurisdiction to register the documents in question. No landor any property was included in the documents within the jurisdiction ofBegumgonj Sub-registrar—Held: The Sub-registrar of Begumgonj had no legal jurisdictionand authority to register the documents. Section-47

Whether thetitle of the defendants related back to the date of execution of theBainapatra. In the instant case the Bainapatra deeds are alleged to have beenexecuted on 22.10.1971 and 13.12.1971. The Kabala deed of the plaintiff wasexecuted and registered on 15.6,1971 and the Kabala deed of the defendants wereexecuted and registered on 31.8.1977. The execution and registration of theKabala deed of the plaintiff was earlier in point of time then the executionand registration of the kabalas of the defendants and the right and the titleof the defendants over the land in dispute will not relate back to the execuHon of the alleged Bainapatra—relied on 44 DLR (AD) 176. [Para-25]
