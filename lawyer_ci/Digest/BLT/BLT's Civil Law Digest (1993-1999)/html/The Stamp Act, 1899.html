The Stamp Act, 1899

 

Section-18

The very act of revalidating or re-stamping the power of attorney is defendant upon reciprocal arrangement. [Para-6]

Molay Behari Biswas Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh 5 BLT (AD)-109

 

Section-35

A failure to comply with the stamp duty as provided under the Stamp Act may entail penalty but the instrument cannot be thrown away for want of stamp as inadmissible in evidence not being duly stamped. [Para-22]

Abdul Karim & Ors. Vs. Md Serajuddin Ahmed & Ors 7 BLT (AD)-160.
