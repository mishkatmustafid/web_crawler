The Waqf Ordinance

 

Section- 32

While proceeding for removal of the Mutwalli Pending there is no provision to appoint a 3rd party to manage the waqf or to take ad-interim action till disposal of the proceeding. [Para-8]

Abdul Khaleque Shah Vs. Administrator of Waqf & Ors. 5 BLT (HCD)-149.

 

Section-64

Section 64 does not give an aggrieved person a remedy before eviction.

M. Shahidullah Vs. Abdus Sobhan Talukder & Ors. 5 BLT (AD)-83.
