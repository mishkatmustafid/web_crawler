Town Improvement Act, 1953

 

Section- 101(1)

Under Section 101(1) of the Town Improvement Act, 1953 it is the absolute right of RAJUK to deal with the property acquired for them. The release in favour of the owners was rightly done. [Para-6]

Court of Wards Bhawal Raj Estate Vs. RAJUK 7 BLT (AD)-304.
