(Emergency) Requisition of Property Act, 1948

 

Section-3

Under the (Emergency) Requisition of property Act, 1948 when the notice under Section 3 is served upon the owner of the land and pursuant to that advance and or partial compensation is paid and the possession of the land is taken over by the Government and given it to the requiring body the owner of the land requisitioned will loose all his right over the land save the right to have compensation and cannot question the manner of use and utilization of the land by the requiring body.

Arbi Khanom & Ors. Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh 8 BLT (HCD)-333
