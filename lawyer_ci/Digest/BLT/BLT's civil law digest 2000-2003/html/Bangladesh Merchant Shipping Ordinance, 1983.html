Bangladesh Merchant Shipping Ordinance, 1983

 

Sections-477 and 479

Since the plaintiff has not been discharged earlier by the owner or master he is entitled to his salary/wages and other allowances as per contract till he is signed off from the vessel—the plaintiff has been able to prove his case and accordingly he is entitled to get the decree.

Volodymyr Portnov Vs, M.V. Terpsichore & Ors 8 BLT(HCD)-187.
