Bengal Tenancy Act, 1885

 

Section-l03 (B)

The presumption of the C. S. Khatian under Section 103(B) of the Bengal Tenancy Act is that every entry in a record of rights finally published shall be the evidence of the matter referred to in such entry and shall be presumed to be correct until it is proved to be incorrect.

Hriday Chandra Dey & Anr. Vs. Probat Dey & Ors. 10 BLT(AD)-137.
