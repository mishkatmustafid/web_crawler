Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education Comilla(Managing Committee of the recognizable Non-Government Schools) Regulations,1997

 

Regulation-7

Without giving anyOpportunity of hearing

We could not find any authority in support ofthe action taken by respondent 3, the Additional Deputy Commissioner (Revenue)retiring the headmaster and asking him to hand over the charge of the office. Ile may be the ex-officio Chairman of the managing committee. But he has got nopower to direct the Headmaster either to retire or ask to hand over chargeindependent of the managing committee, it is on record that the managingcommittee considered the long devoted service of the headmaster to the schoolin three meetings in his presence and in the last meeting when the resolutionextending the service was adopted he did not attend, lie cannot supersede theresolution of such managing committee by executive flat. His action smacks ofbureaucratic mindset, which cannot be endorsed.

Iqbal Ahmed Chowdhury & Ors. Vs Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education & Ors. 11 BLT (HCD)-284.
