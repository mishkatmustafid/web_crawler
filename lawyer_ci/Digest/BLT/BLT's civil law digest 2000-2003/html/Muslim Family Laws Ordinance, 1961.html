Muslim Family Laws Ordinance, 1961

 

Section-7(l)

In this case, as the nocite of talak was given on 02.03.1989 to the Administrator,Dhaka City Corporation and also to the petitioner talak has on expiry of 90 days from the date of receipt of notice by the Administrator has taken effect as provided in sub-sectioin (3) of Section-7 of the Ordinance.

Md. Ataul Hoquc Vs. Anwr A. Karim 8 BLT (AD)-30.

 

Section-7(1)

Talak was pronounced on 08.09.1999 whereas the notice served on 14.01.1999 to the complainant but notice is to be served upon the chairman of the Arbitration Council concerned- there was not legal divorce on 14.1.1999.

Md. Giasuddin Khan Vs. Miss Beauty Begum & Ors. 8BLT(HCD)-328
