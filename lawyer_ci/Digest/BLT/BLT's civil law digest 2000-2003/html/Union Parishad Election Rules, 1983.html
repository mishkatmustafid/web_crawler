Union Parishad Election Rules, 1983

 

Recounting of the ballot papers.

Election tribunal—recounting is not to be granted as a matter of course. It is only to be allowed when the tribunal is satisfied on the basis of evidence before it that recounting is indispensable necessary for complete and effectual justice.

Abdul Lafit Bepari Vs. Md. Nurul Islam Hawlader & Ors 8BLT(AD)-233
