Voluntary Social Welfare Agencies (Registration and Control) Ordinance, 1961

 

Section-9(l)(2) read with

Foreign Donation (Voluntary Activities) Regulation Ordinance, 1978

Since GSS has been registered under Voluntary Social Welfare Agencies (Registration and Control) Ordinance. 1961 and also under the Foreign Donations (Voluntary Activities) Regulation Ordinance, 1978 it was amenable to the control and supervision of respondent No. 1 Director General of the Department of Social Services of the Government.

Justice (Retired) K. M. Sohhan & Anr. Vs. Department of Social Service & Ors. 9BLT (AD)-143
