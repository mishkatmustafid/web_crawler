Bangladesh Merchant Shipping Ordinance, 1983

 

Sections-477and 479

Since the plaintiff has not been dischargedearlier by the owner or master he is entitled to his salary/wages and otherallowances as per contract till he is signed off from the vessel—the plaintiffhas been able to prove his case and accordingly he is entitled to get thedecree.
