Bangladesh Oil, Gas and Mineral Corporation Employees’ Service Regulations, 1988

Regulation- 13

An Agreed and Modified Order

The operative part of the judgment of the High Court Division is modified to the extent that the impugned orders of promotion will remain undisturbed and respondent No. I Corporation (now petitioner) is directed to consider the case of promotion of the respondents (Petitioners in Writ Petition) restoring their seniority by making a gradation list of the Corporation and those on deputation to the BAPEX in accordance with regulation 13 of the Service Regulations, 1988.

Petrobangla Vs. A. N. M. Belayet Hossain & Ors. 7 BLT (AD)-325.
