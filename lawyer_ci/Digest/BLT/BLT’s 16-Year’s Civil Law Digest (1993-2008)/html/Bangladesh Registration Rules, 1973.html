Bangladesh Registration Rules, 1973

 

Rule-48

Held, I am of the view that non-compliance with the provision of rule 48 of the Registration Rules 1973 in registering the kabala in question by the Sub-Registrar made the same void. Arid the said noncompliance also creates a prima-facie doubt about the execution and registration of the Kabala by the plaintiff.

Foez Ahmed Vs. Joynal Abedin & Ors 12 BLT (HCD) 476.
