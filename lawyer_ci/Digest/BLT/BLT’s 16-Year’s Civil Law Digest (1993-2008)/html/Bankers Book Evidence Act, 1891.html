Bankers Book Evidence Act, 1891

 

Section-6

If anyparty to a legal proceeding made any application to a Court or to a Judge, suchCourt for Judge may direct for inspection of those documents or to take copiesof any entries of the bankers books for the purpose of such proceeding; It isreferred in the law that when a legal proceeding is pending, Only in connectionof such legal proceeding and on such prayer, the court may order for inspectionof such documents or for taking copies of entries but there is no provisioneither in section 5 or 6 of the Bankers Books Procedure enabling any policeofficer or an officer under the Bureau of Anti-Corruption for obtainingpermission from the Court of Sessions for seizure of any banking document forthe purpose of any inquiry before initiation of any legal proceeding.

Islami BankBangladesh Ltd. Vs. Dist. Anti-Corruption Office & Anr. 11 BLT (HCD)-246.
