Regulation-7

Without giving anyOpportunity of haring

We couldnot find any authority in support of the action taken by respondent 3, theAdditional Deputy Commissioner(Revenue) retiring the headmaster and asking himto hand Over the charge of the office. He may be the ex-officio Chairman of themanaging committee. But he has got no power to direct the Headmaster either toretire or ask to hand over charge independent of the managing committee, it ison record that the managing committee considered the long devoted service ofthe headmaster to the school in three meetings in his presence and in the lastmeeting when the resolution extending the service was adopted he did notattend. He cannot supersede the resolution of such managing committee byexecutive fiat. His action smacks of bureaucratic mindset, which cannot heendorsed.
