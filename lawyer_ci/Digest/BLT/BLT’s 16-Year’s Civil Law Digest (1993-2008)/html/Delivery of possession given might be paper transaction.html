Delivery Date: 1970-01-01

 

Delivery of possession given might be paper transaction

Decree passed in a suit ex parte — in execution case delivery of possession was given but as because there is doubt as regards serving of summons upon the defendants, delivery of possession given might be paper transaction.

Md. Abul Hossain & Ors Vs Nironjan Mondal & Ors. 2 BLT (HCD)-179.
