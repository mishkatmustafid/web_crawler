East Pakistan (Land and Buildings) Administration Disposal Order, 1966

 

Article—4(3)

Sub-Article 3 of Article 4 of East Pakistan (Land and Buildings) Administration Disposal Order, 1966 enshrines that notwithstanding anything contained in any other law for the time being in force or in any agreement, a person to whom any property is leased out or let out under Subparagraph (2) shall not acquire any right of occupation in such property and shall not be entitled to hold over after the expiry of period of lease.

Sree Narayan Chandra Basak & Ors Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh & Ors 15 BLT (HCD) 506.
