Environment Conservation Act, 1995

Section-6A

The scope of Section 6A is very wide. Not only polythene but any commodity which may appear to be hazardous for environment, the Government may interfere under this Section in respect of production etc. and may impose conditions which the concern person(s) must obey.

Md. Solaiman Khan & Ors Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh 12 BLT (HCD)342
