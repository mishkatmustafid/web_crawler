General Principles of Seniority, 1970

Rule-(11)-B read with Fundamental Rules

Rule-30

Though the promotion could not be claimed as of right but the case of the appellant was not considered when his juniors were awarded promotion, more so, when the appellant was not found to be unfit for promotion. Thus the authority ,had acted illegally and without lawful authority in not considering the appellant’s case of promotion though he was not found to be unfit for the purpose.

Md. Nurul Hoque Miah Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh & Ors. 14 BLT (AD)53.
