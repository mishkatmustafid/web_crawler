High Court Civil Rules and Orders

Rule 466(2)

Orders directing anything to be done by aparty must be communicated to it.

The Ruleprovides that orders directing anything to be done by a party or his Advocatemust be communicated to and signed by the party or his Advocate. If an order ofCourt affecting one of the pillars of the suit such as transfer of the casefrom one Court to another or from one place to another is meant forcommunication, it must be so communicated before the suit procedures further.

Md. Anwarul Haque& Ors. Vs Hossain Ali Matbar & Ors. 8BLT(HCD)-36.
