Jananirapatta Bishes Bidhan Ain (রহিত করণ) 2002 [Act]

 

Section —42

The Provisions of Section 3 of the Jananirapatta Bishes Bidhan Ain (রহিত করণ) 2002 (Act VI of 2002) kept all pending proceedings alive and all the tribunals setup under the Act were kept functional. In other words the Ain is still in full force in respect of pending proceedings under the said Ain.

Mamunur Rashid Mamun Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh & Ors 16 BLT (AD)226.
