Law and Order disruption Crimes (Speedy Trial) Act, 2002

Section 4(1)

PW- 1, PW-2, PW-4 and PW-5 are the most natural witnesses who were present at time of the occurrence and who are the victim of the incident. The evidence of these witnesses are uniform and consistent in all material aspects. I therefore, find no reasons to disbelieve the prosecution witnesses who are probable and competent.

Salim Vs The State 14 BLT (HCD) 81.
