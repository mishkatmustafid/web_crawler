Rajshahi Krishi Unnayan Bank Karmachari Chakuri Probidhanmala, 1988 [As Amended]

Rule-39(8)

Held: We have gone through the judgment of the Administrative Tribunal wherein it has been found that 717 days was consumed for the disposal of the proceeding though time limit was 180 days. This proceeding ended on 13.10.199 1 and the time limit that was there in the rule was withdrawn with effect from 3.2.1992 and from this the Administrative Tribunal found that there was no wrong in the judgment of the Tribunal in holding that the case is barred by limitation. We have considered the judgment of both the Tribunals and also the admitted position that there was approval of amendment of the rule on 3.2.1992 which has no retrospective effect and thereby it appears that the case is barred by limitation.

Rajshahi Krishi Unnayan Bank Vs. Md. Ahsanul Kabir 10 BLT (AD)-37.
