Recognized Non-Government Secondary Schools Regulation, 1977

Whether an executive committee after beingelected has to be approved by the Board

Learnedappellate court below allowed the prayer for temporary and mandatory injunctionon the ground that the Managing Committee which was elected in 1995 is not avalid committee in as much as the said committee has not been approved by theBoard—There is no such provision in the regulation which requires that anexecutive committee after being elected has to be approved by the Board.

Dr. Habibur Rahman M.P. & Ors Vs. Most.Shamsun Naher 6 BLT (HCD)-13.
