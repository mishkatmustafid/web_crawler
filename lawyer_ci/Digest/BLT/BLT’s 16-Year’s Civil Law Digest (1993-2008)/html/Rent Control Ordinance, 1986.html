Rent Control Ordinance, 1986

Section-19(2)

Under 5. 19 (2) of the Premises Rent Control Ordinance a tenant is permitted to deposit rent in case of bonafide doubt or dispute as to the persons entitled to receive the rent—There is no wrong in the order of the High Court Division allowing the tenants application for making deposit of rent.

Al-Haj Mirjahan Vs. M/S. Golden Biscuit 1 BLT(AD)-42
