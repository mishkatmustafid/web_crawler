Sale of Goods Act, 1930 (III of 1930)

Chapter VI Section-61 (2)

Section 61 (2) provides that the court may award interest at such rate as it thinks fit on the amount of the price to the seller in a suit by him for the amount of the price from the date of the tender of the goods or from the date of which the price is payable.

Sonali Bonk Vs M/S. Karnaphuli Works Ltd. 2BLT (AD)-78.
