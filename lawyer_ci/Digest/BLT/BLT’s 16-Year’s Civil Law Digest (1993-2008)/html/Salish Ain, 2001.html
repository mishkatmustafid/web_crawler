Salish Ain, 2001

SalishAin, 2001

 Section-20

Order of injunction

Earlierthe Arbitration Act, 1940, in its Section 41 provides for application of theCode of Civil Procedure, which obviously includes the power to issue an orderof injunction. The Salish Ain, 2001 does not contain such a provision. It wouldrather appear that such a provision was consciously omitted. The purpose, as itappears, is clear, to push ahead the arbitral proceeding without any hindrance.
