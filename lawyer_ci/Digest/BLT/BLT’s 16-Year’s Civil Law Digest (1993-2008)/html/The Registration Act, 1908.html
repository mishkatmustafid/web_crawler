The Registration Act, 1908

Section-28

The land in question is situated within Laxmipur sub-registry whether the Begumgonj Sub-registrar had jurisdiction to register the documents in question. No land or any property was included in the documents within the jurisdiction of Begumgonj Sub registrar—Held: The Sub-registrar of Begumgonj had no legal jurisdiction and authority to register the documents.

Barada Sundari Vs. Makhan Chandra Das 6 BLT (HCD)-210.

Section-47

Whether the title of the defendants related back to the date of execution of the Bainapatra

In the instant case the Bainapatra deeds are alleged to have been executed on 22.10.1971 and 13.12.1971. The Kabala deed of the plaintiff was executed and registered on 15.6.1971 and the Kabala deed of the defendants were executed and registered on 31.8.1977. The execution and registration of the Kabala deed of the plaintiff was earlier in point of time then the execution and registration of the kabalas of the defendants and the right and the title of the defendants over the land in dispute will not relate back to the execution of the alleged Bainapatra— relied on 44 DLR (AD) 176.

Zamiruddin Ahmed Vs. Md. Ziaul Haq & Ors. 6 BLT (HCD)-5.
