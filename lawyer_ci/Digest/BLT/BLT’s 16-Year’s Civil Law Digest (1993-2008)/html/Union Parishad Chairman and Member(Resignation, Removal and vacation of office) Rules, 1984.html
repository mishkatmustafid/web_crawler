Union Parishad Chairman and Member(Resignation, Removal and vacation of office) Rules, 1984

Rule-12(1) read with Local Government Union Parishad Ordinance, 1983 Section- 12(2)

Notice of no-confidence

Authority has followed the procedure prescribed by the Rules and the petitioner was given chance to show cause and also asked to be present in the special meeting. The Authority has removed the petitioner under Rule 12(1) of the Union Parishad Chairman and Member (Resignation, Removal and Vacation of Office) Rules, 1984 in which recording of reasons is not necessary at all.

Md. Habibur Rahman Vs. Govt. of Bangladesh & Ors 16 BLT (AD)163.
