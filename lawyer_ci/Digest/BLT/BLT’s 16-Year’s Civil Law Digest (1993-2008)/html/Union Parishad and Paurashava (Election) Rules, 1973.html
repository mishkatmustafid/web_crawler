Union Parishad and Paurashava (Election) Rules, 1973 (as amended)

Rule—62

The Election Tribunal was not justified to delegate power to a Magistrate to recount votes and was not justified in interfering with the result of election upon consideration of the report submitted by the Magistrate.

Rafiqul Islam & Ors. Vs. Md. Abdul Jabbar Sarker & Ors 14 BLT (HCD)352.
