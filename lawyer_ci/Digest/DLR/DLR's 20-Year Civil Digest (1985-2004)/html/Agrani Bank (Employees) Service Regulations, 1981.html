Agrani Bank (Employees) Service Regulations, 1981

 

 

Regulation 28(1)(e)

Second show cause notice-Since the departmental enquiry against the employee was held and his order of dismissal was passed on the basis of report of enquiry after the present Regulation came into force, bis case will be governed by the Regulation and he will be entitled to a second show cause notice before his dismissal from service. This having not been done the dismissal is illegal.

Fazlul Karim vs Agrani Bank and others 45 DLR 375.
