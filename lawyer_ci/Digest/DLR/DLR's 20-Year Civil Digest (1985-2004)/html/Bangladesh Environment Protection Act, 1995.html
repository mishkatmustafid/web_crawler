Bangladesh Environment Protection Act, 1995

 

Sections 4(3), 8, 10 & 11-

Proviso to sub­section 3 of section 4 of the Paribesh Ain has made it mandatory upon the Director-General to give written notice to the owner of the industry-factory concerned giving him chance to take necessary step to rectify the environmental hazard.

Abu Jafar Md Nurul Islam vs DG, Department of Environment and others 56 DLR 1.

Section 14 –

In view of the finding that the impugned order was passed .at the behest of the Minister-in-charge in clear violation of the mandatory provisions of law, it would have served no purpose if an appeal was filed to the appellate authority. Moreso, when the appellate authority has got no power to pass any interim order which was necessary.
