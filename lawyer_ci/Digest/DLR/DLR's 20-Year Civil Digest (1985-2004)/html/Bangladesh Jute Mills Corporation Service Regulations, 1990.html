Bangladesh Jute Mills Corporation Service Regulations, 1990

 

 

Rules 43(5)(6)-

The supply of enquiry report along with the second show cause notice is not an idle formality but the requirement of law which is also in consonance with the provision of principle of natural justice.

Golam Mostafa (Md) and 2 ors vs Bangladesh Jute Mills Corporation and others 52 DLR 299.
