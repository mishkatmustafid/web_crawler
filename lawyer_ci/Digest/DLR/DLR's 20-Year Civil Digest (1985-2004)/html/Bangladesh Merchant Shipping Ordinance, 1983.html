Bangladesh Merchant Shipping Ordinance, 1983

 

 

Section 43-

Jurisdiction of High Court in matters concerning marine insurance or maritime assurance arising out of such contracts and claims arising out of a marine mortgage is covered by section 11 of the Admiralty Courts Act of 1861 and by section 43 of tlte Bangladesh Merchant Shipping Ordinance.

Bengal Liner Ltd Dhaka vs Sadharon Bima Corporation 42 DLR 281.
