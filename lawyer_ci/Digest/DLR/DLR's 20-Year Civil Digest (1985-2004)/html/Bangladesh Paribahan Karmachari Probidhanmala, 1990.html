Regulation 55(2)-

Though on the face of it the impugned order removing the respondent from service appears to be innocuous one, the decision of the High Court Division that Regulation 55(2) was used as a camouflage for hiding the real purpose of the employer, that of removing the former by way of a major punishment without any proceeding, does not call for interference.
