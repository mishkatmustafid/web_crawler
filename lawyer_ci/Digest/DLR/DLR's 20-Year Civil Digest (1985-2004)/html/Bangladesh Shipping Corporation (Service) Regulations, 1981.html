Bangladesh Shipping Corporation (Service) Regulations, 1981

 

Regulation 12(a)-

Termination of service of confirmed officer of the Corporation is nothing but a departmental administrative action as required to be done by the Corporation itself in accordance with Regulation No. 12(a). There is no omnibus power given to the concerned Ministry of the Government to give any direction or pass any order upon the functions of the Corporation prescribed by Rules and regulated by Regulations of the corporation. Government cannot act beyond the provisions of President's Order No. 10 of 1972 and the prescribed Rules and Regulations.

Bangladesh Shipping Corporation vs Raqibuddin Ahmed 46 DLR 470.
