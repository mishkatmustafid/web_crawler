Bangladesh Transfer of Immovable Property (Temporary Provisions) Order, 1972

 

Article 6 –

Mere non-impleading of the Government in the suit does not non-suit the plaintiffs automatically.

Abdul Quayuam Khan vs Abu Yusuf Mridha 51 DLR 386.

Article 6-

Where in the suit no public interest is involved, Government is not necessary party. Moreover, plea of non-joinder of necessary party need be raised at the earliest opportunity.

Abdul Quayum Khan and others vs Md Abu Yousuf Mirdha and others 52 DLR (AD) 48.
