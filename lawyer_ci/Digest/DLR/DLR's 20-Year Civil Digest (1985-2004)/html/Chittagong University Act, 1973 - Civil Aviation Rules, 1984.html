Chittagong University Act, 1973

Section 49(3)-

The educational institution where the maintenance of discipline is essential for the orderly conduct in the institution the person in charge must be given the fullest authority to correct those who are found guilty for breach of discipline in the same manner as a parent or guardian may chastise their wards for their rectification. But the authority is not free from acting in the manner which excludes every reasonable possibility of unfair action being taken.

Shahryar Murshed vs Principal, Chittagong Medical College and others 48 DLR 482.

Chittagong Water Supply & Sewerage Authority Service Regulations, 1968

Regulation 16(a)(ii)-

For determination of seniority or inter-se seniority length of service would be counted from the date of appointment in a particular post and not according to the date of one's confirmation in the post.

AKM Fazlullah vs Chittagong Water Supply and Sewerage Authority WASA Bhaban and others 49 DLR 445.

Citizenship Act [II of 1951)

 

Section 4-

Birth of the petitioners in Geneva Camp or their continued residence in Geneva Camp do not affect the citizenship by birth acquired under section 4 of the Act and as such they are also Bangladeshi citizens by birth.

Abid Khan and others (Md) vs Government of Bangladesh and others 55 DLR 318

Civil Aviation Authority Ordinance [XXXVIII of 1985]

Section 5-

There is nothing in the Civil Aviation Authority Ordinance that Chairman and Members of the Board of the Authority were vested with any portion of the sovereign functions of the Government. They are merely managing a civil aviation business of the Government.

Abdur Rahman (Md) vs Group Captain (Retd) Shamim Hossain and others 49 DLR 628.
