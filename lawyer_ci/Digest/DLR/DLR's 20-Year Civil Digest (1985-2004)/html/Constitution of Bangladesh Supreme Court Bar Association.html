Constitutionof Bangladesh Supreme Court Bar Association


Article 3 –

Thepurpose and object of the Bar Association are for giving facilities to itsmembers for conducting the profession. It is a welfare association. It cannotinterfere with the private conduct of a member.

Thepost of Minister or Vice President held by the petitioner had nothing to dowith his profession as a member of the Bar Association. The Association cannotimpose any restriction towards holding of such post. The Bar Council did not atall consider that the resolution directing the petitioner to tender resignationfrom a post which has no connection with the profession was not binding on thepetitioner and for non–compliance with the same his membership cannot beceased.

Moudud Ahmedvs Bangladesh Bar Council 46 DLR 71.
