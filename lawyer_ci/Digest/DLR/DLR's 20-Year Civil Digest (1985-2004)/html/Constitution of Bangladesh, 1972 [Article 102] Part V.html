Articles 102 and 152(2)–

A company isalso a person under section 3(39) of the General Clauses Act read with Article152(2) of the Constitution. The word 'person' occurring in Article 102includes, by operation of section 3(39) of the General Clauses Act, a companyas well.

So, undersection 3(39) of the General Clauses Act read with Article 152(2) of theConstitution a company is also a person. Moreover, it appears to us that theword, "person", has been used in unqualified term thereby including ajuristic person. If it was intended to limit its meaning to its narrower senseto mean an "individual" to the exclusion of any juristic person itwould have been done in explicit language. In absence of any explicit languageto exclude a juristic person from the word "person" we are inclinedto hold that the word "person" occurring in Article 102 includes, byoperation of section 3(39) of the General Clauses Act, a company as well.

ConforceLimited vs Titas Gas Transmission 42 DLR 33.
