Whether aperson is under a contract of service or contract for service is a question offact and not a question of law and is to be determined on consideration of thefacts and circumstances of each case.

Section 13–

The authorof a work shall be the first owner of the copyright in the work subject to sameconditions, one of which is that a work made in the course of author'semployment under a contract of service or apprenticeship when none of the otherconditions applies, the employer of the employed person shall, in the absenceof any agreement to the contrary, be the first owner of the copyright therein.
