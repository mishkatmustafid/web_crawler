Drugs Act [XXIIIof 1940]


Section5(1)–

Manufacturersof  Non­ Ayurvedic and non–Unanimedicines must get a licence for the purpose and also must register theirmedicine with Licensing Authority. Manufac­turer of Ayurvedic and Unanimedicines are only to register their medicines with the Licensing Authority andnothing more under section 5(1).

Ruhul Aminvs Director, Drug Administration 39 DLR 158.
