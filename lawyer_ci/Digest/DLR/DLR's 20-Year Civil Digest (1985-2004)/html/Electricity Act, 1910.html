Electricity Act, 1910

 

ElectricityAct [IX of 1910]


Section 24–

Disconnectionof electricity line– Question of giving notice– Materials on record show thatthe Power Development Board removed its transformer from the appellant'scontrol in order to forestall reconnection of electric line that the appellantwas doing on his own. There having been nothing to show that the Board everofficially reconnected the line after it was disconnected, the appellant cannotlegitimately claim 10 days' notice as provided under section 24 of the Act.

GiasuddinBhuiyan vs Chairman, PDE 45 DLR (AD) 29.
