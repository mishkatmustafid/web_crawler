Emergency (Land and Building) Requisition Ordinance, 1982

 

Emergency(Land and Building) Requisition Ordinance [II of 1982]


Sections 5 & 44–

Section 5 ofthe Ordinance provides that the decision of the government shall be theconclusive evidence that the property is needed for a public purpose. The HighCourt Division is correct in saying that if from the pleading it can be seenthat the purpose does not appear to be in public interest the Civil Court'sjurisdiction is not ousted by section 44 of the Ordinance.

Ali Jan Khanvs Bangladesh 37 DLR (AD) 161.

 

Section 44–

Property hasbeen acquired for valid purpose, the Civil Court has no jurisdiction toquestion the acquisition.
