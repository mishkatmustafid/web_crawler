Extradition Act, 1974

 

ExtraditionAct [LVIII of 1974]


Section 4–

Thegovernment has sufficient legal authority to extradite Anup Chetia to India inresponse to the right of India to demand extradition of Anup Chetia.

Saiful IslamDilder vs Government of Bangladesh and others 50 DLR 318.

 

Section 6–

A 'fugitiveoffender can be extradited from Bangladesh to the United Kingdom in the manneras stated in section 6 of the Act.

Abdul AbedinAbdul vs State 53 DLR 109.

 

Sections 6 & 12–

Inconsidering the prayer for bail of a fugitive offender arrested or detainedunder the Extradition Act, provision of section 497 of the Code of CriminalProcedure shall have to be considered.
