Oath ofOffice (Union Parishads) Rules, 1983


Rule 2–

The failureof the Chairman and Members to take oath can be inferred only when the DeputyCommissioner fixes a date of oath within 30 days of Gazette notification andthe Chairman or Members, without good cause shown, fail to take oath on thedate fixed.

Syed HedayetAli vs Chief Election Commissioner and others 50 DLR {AD) 89.
