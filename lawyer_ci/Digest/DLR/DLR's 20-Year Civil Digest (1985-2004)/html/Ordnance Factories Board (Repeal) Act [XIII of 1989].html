OrdnanceFactories Board (Repeal) Act [XIII of 1989]


Section 2(2)(Cha)–

Since theterms and conditions of service have not been changed or altered, the employeescontinue to be governed by the Rules i.e. Ordnance Factories Board Ordnance,1961 under which they used to be governed before the repealing Act came intoforce.
