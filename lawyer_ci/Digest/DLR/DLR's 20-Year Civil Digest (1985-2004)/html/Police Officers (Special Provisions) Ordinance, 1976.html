Police Officers (Special Provisions) Ordinance, 1976

 

 

PoliceOfficers (Special Provisions) Ordinance [LXXXIV of 1976]


Section 3—

Punishmentlike dismissal or removal in respect of a police personnel like the respondent,a Sub-Inspector of Police, which can be awarded by the Inspector General ofPolice, can also be awarded under regulation No. 858 (a) (2) by theSuperintendent of Police.
