Rules 4(b)(c),4(6)—

Power DevelopmentBoard disqualified the petitioner from promotion to a higher post merely on theground of academic disqualification (he was not a graduate). This the Board wasnot entitled to do under the rules of the Bangladesh Power Development Board(Employees) Service Rules, 1982.
