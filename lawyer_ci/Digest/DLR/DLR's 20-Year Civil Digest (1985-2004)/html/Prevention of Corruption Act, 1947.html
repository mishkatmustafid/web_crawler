Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947

 

Preventionof Corruption Act [II of 1947]

 

Section 5(l)(d) & (e)—

Embezzlementof State money by a person who was the President of the country and using thesame for his personal benefit or living or having properties disproportionateto his known source of income will definitely come within the ambit of moralturpitude.

HussainMohammad Ershad vs Zahedul Islam Khan 54 DLR (AD) 1.
