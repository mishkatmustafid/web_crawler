Printing Presses and Publication (Declaration and Registration) Act, 1973

 

PrintingPresses and Publication (Declaration and Registration) Act [XXIII of 1973]

 

Declarationin respect of authentication of a newspaper can be done as much by anAdditional District Magistrate as the District Magistrate under section 12 ofthe Act. By virtue of the definition an Additional District Magistrate is alsoincluded in the term District Magistrate.

Waliul BariChowdhury vs District Magistrate, Kushtia 38 DLR (AD) 256.
