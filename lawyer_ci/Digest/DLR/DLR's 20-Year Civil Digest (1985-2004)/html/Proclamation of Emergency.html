Proclamationof Emergency


—Proclamationof Emergency made on 6th September, 1965 was revoked on 16th February 1969 butsimultaneously the Enemy Property (Continuance of Emergency Provisions)Ordinance (I of 1969) was promulgated. Thereafter the provisions of thisOrdinance (I of 1969) was repealed by the promulgation of the Enemy Property(Continuance of Emergency Provisions) (Repeal) Ordinance, 1974 followed byEnemy Property (Continuance of Emergency Provisions) (Repeal) Act, (XLV of1974).

PriyatoshTalukdar vs Asstt. Custodian 39 DLR (AD) 178.
