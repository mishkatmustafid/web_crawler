Rajshahi University Act, 1973

 

RajshahiUniversity Act [XXXVI of 1973]

 

Sections 9 and 55(3)—

TheAppointing Authority has power under section 16 of the General Clauses Act tosuspend, dismiss and remove the Vice—Chancellor, although there is no specificprovision in the Rajshahi University Act itself for removing theVice—Chancellor of the University. The procedure laid down in section 5(3) ofthe Rajshahi University Act 1973 is not applicable to the Vice—Chancellor forifhe is taken to be an officer of the University then as per section 9 of thesaid Act the Chancellor should also be deemed to be an officer of theUniversity to be dealt with in accordance with the procedure laid down insection 55(3) of the same Act which is nothing but an absurdity.

Professor DrMd Yusuf Ali vs Chancellor of Rajshahi University, Rajshahi & others 50 DLR1.
