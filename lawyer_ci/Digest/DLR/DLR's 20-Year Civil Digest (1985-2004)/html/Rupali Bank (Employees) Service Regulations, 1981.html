Rupali Bank(Employees) Service Regulations, 1981


Regulations 13(2) & 28—

Theobjective to get rid of unwanted employees without taking recourse to procedureof disciplinary action is likely to be frustrated if termination is madedependent on actual receipt of notice pay.

Rupali Bankand another vs Md Solaiman and others 49 DLR 526.

 

Regulation 16—

Duty to paypensions to a person who has worked for the stipulated period of time remainsan undercharged contractual obligation—Where the employee retires or isterminated there must exist provision for payment of pension, describing themode and timing of payment of pension to terminated employees, else courts willimply terms to that effect.

MA Jalil vsRupali Bank Ltd & others 54 DLR 566.
