Securities & Exchange Ordinance, 1969

 

Securities& Exchange Ordinance [XVII of 1969]


Section 19—

Informationand documents called for from petitioner do not relate to performance of anyfunction under the Ordinance but relates to his business of dealing in shareswith his customers. This does not entitle him to take shelter under theprovision of section 19 of the Ordinance.

KhurshidAlam vs Securities and Exchange Commission and others 51 DLR 534.
