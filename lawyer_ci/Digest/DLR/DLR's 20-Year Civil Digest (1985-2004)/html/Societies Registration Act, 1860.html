Societies Registration Act, 1860

 

SocietiesRegistration Act [XXI of 1860]


Section 20—

Charitablesociety can invest its unspent money for getting more money to be spent incharity and when there is absolutely no bar under the Societies RegistrationAct and when the Memorandum of Association of a society has authorised the sameto invest their money not immediately required the same may be invested.

BRAC andothers vs Professor Mozaffar Ahmed and others 54 DLR (AD) 36.
