Sonali BankEmployees Service Regulations, 1981

 

Section 1—

Theclassification of officers and other employees as given in Schedule I of theSonali Bank Employees Service Regulations, 1981 is nothing but an internalagreement made by the Bank for staff management for the purpose of theirinternal administration only. This classification made in the schedule has nothingto do with the definition of worker.
