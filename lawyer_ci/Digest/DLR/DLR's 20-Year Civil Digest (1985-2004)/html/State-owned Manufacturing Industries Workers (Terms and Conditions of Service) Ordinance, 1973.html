State-owned Manufacturing Industries Workers (Terms and Conditions of Service) Ordinance, 1973

 

State-ownedManufacturing Industries Workers (Terms and Conditions of Service) Ordinance[XXIII of 1973]


Section 3—

As paymentof bonus to employees is an incentive for service rendered by them, suchpayment of bonus as directed by the Government is not dependent on profit.
