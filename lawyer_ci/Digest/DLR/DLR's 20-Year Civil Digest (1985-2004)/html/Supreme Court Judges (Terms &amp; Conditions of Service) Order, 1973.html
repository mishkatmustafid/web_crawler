Supreme Court Judges (Terms & Conditions of Service) Order, 1973

 

SupremeCourt Judges (Terms & Conditions of Service) Order [PO No. 21 of 1973]


Article 10—

Salary ofSupreme Court judges being exempted from taxation it could not be included forthe purpose of taxation while computing their total income. This position couldnot be affected by the notification issued in exercise of powers under theIncome Tax Act, for, the Act cannot control the provision of the President'sOrder that has provided for the exemption and achieved the status ofsub-constitutional legislation.

Commissionerof Taxes vs Shahabuddin Ahmed 42 DLR (AD) 162.
