Trade MarkRules


Rule 23—

A notice tothe concerned applicant by the Registrar should be impliedly read in therelevant section of the Act and rule of the Trade Marks Rules, 1963 framedthereunder to avoid abuse of power and for the sake of public interest.

Solaiman Ali(Md) vs Md Abul Hussain and another 54 DLR 294.
