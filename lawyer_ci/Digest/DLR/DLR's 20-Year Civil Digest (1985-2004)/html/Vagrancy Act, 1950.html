Vagrancy Act[LXXIV of 1950]


Section 2(a)—

Mere roamingaround in any area would not bring the sex—workers within the mischief ofsection 2(9) of Vagrancy Act so as to adjudicate them as vagrant unless one isfound to be a vagrant and other conditions for the purpose are complied with.
