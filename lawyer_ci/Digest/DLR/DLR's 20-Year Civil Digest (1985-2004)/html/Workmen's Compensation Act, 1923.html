Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923

 

Workmen'sCompensation Act [VIII of 1923]


Section 11—

The sectionenjoins service of notice upon the employer and not upon any other person, andthe worker sustaining injury is to submit himself for medical examination by aqualified doctor of the employer.

Amina Khatunvs Mis Quick Ways Agency 43 DLR 325.

 

Section 30—

Where anorder of the Commissioner for Workmen's Compensation does not involve asubstantial question of law no appeal under this section of the Workmen'sCompensation Act lies.

Amina Khatun& others vs Mis Quick Ways Agency 43 DLR 325.
