Order XXIX (CPC)

 

Suit by or against Corporation

Or. 29, r. 1 (High Court Rules, Part I, r. 4)

Verification of affidavit—where the petitioner is a public trust.

Where a petitioner is a public trust a writ petition on behalf of the public trust when verified by the Estate Officer of the trust who is conversant with the facts of the case is a substantial compliance with the provision of rule 4 of Part 1 of the High Court Rules.

Kumudini Welfare Trust of Bengal Vs. The Province of East Pakistan (1961) 13 DLR 693.
