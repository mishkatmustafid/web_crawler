Parsi Marriage and Divorce Act

(III of 1936)

 

S.32 (g). Husband filed a petition for divorce against the wife in High Court of West Pakistan, on ground of desertion by wife — Wife, however, already obtained an ex pane decree for judicial separation from Bombay High Court— decree for judicial separation, held, not judgment-in-rem.

Farokh Homi Irani vs. Nargish Farokh (1963) 15 DLR (WP) 105.
