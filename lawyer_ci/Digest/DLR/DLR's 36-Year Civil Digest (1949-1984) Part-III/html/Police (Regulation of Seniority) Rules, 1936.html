Police (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1936

 

Not of same nature as other Rules governing conduct of officers—Vested right to place in seniority list—Superior Courts competent to deal with matter vo1ving interpretation of Seniority Rules—Fit subject for Reference under Art. 162, Constitution of Pakistan, 1956.

Bashir Ahmed Khan Vs Mahammed Ali Khan Chowdhury (1960) 12 DLR (SC) 94.

Seniority of directly recruited officer in Police Service of Pakistan as against a “promoted officer”— Determinable with reference to date of commencement of officiation of former. Ibid.
