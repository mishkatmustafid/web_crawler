Port Act (XV of 1908)

 

S. 6(i) (ii): Petitioner having lost his right to own and possess jetties not entitled to challenge rule 80 of the East Pakistan Port Rules as ultra vires the Provincial Government in writ petition.

High Speed Navigation Co .Vs. East Pakistan Inland Water Transport Authority, (1970) 22 DLR 337.

S. 10(1): Construction of a wall on the river bank within the jurisdiction of the Port by person other than the Port Authority—A nuisance’ affecting lawful use of the bank by the Port Authority and therefore liable to be restrained.

Star Alkaid Jute Millss Ltd. Vs. EPIWTA, (1972) 24 DLR 1.
