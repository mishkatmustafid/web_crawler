President’s Order 36 of 1962

 

Settlement of Disputes (First Elections) (Second Amendment) Order.

If during the course of the trial of an election petition any one of the members of the Tribunal is for any reason unable to attend any sitting of the Tribunal, the trial shall continue before the other two members of the Tribunal.

Mahbubul Huq Vs. Chief Election Commissioner of Pakistan (1968)20 DLR 883.
