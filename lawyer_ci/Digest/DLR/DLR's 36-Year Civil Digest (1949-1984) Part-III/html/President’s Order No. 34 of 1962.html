President’s Order No. 34 of 1962

 

The Order being of the nature altering the Constitution—Court’s right to interpret the Consti0n cannot be taken away by the Order—Order ultra vires.

 

The aspect of the franchise and of the form of Government are fundamental features of a Constitution and to alter them, in order to placate or secure the support of a few persons, would be equivalent not to bringing the given Constitution into force, to bringing into effect an altered or different constitution.

FazIul Quader Chowdhury Vs. Muhammad Abdul Haque, (1966) 18 DLR (SC) 69.
