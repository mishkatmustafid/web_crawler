Proclamation of Independence

 

Proclamation of Independence from Mujib Nagar on 10.4.71.

The Proclamation of Independence made from Mujibnagar on 10th April, 1971, a declaration of the then East Pakistan into a sovereign Independent State. It recited that the Pakistan Government had started an unjust war which led to the making of such declaration of independence.

Jamal Jute Baling Vs. M. Sarkies (1978) 30 DLR (SC) 23.

 

People’s Republic of Bangladesh set up on 26.3.71 after proclaiming independence.

The State of People’ Republic of Bangladesh was established after proclaiming Independence on the 26th March, 1971. The Constitution of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh was enacted by the Constituent Assembly on the 4th November, 1972.

Md. Khalilur Rahaman Vs. State (1979) 31 DLR 50
