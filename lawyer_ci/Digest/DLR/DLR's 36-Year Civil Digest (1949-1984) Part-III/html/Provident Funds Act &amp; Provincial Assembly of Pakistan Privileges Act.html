Provident Funds Act & Provincial Assembly of Pakistan Privileges Act

Citation: 20 DLR (WP) 90

Subject: Provident Funds & Provincial Assembly of Pakistan Privileges

Delivery Date: 1970-01-01

 

 

Provident Funds Act

(XIX OF 1925)

S.5. Nominee entitled.

The words “entitled to the exclusion of all other persons to receive such sum” in sec. 5 of the Provident Funds Act means that the nominee receives as beneficiary and not as a trustee for the benefit of those otherwise entitled to the sum. PLR (1960) 1 WP. 729.

 

Provincial Assembly of Pakistan Privileges Act

(XXXIII of 1964)

S.5. Member of Assembly cannot be dive of his privilege except by his own waiver.

Mian Muhammad Sharif Vs. Member, Election Commission (1968) 20 DLR (WP) 90.
