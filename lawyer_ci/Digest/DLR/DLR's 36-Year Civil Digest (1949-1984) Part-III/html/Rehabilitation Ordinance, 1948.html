S. 11(4): Rehabilitation Re-settlement Scheme (Punjab), Part 1, para 23 and part 11 para 7. Rehabilitation Commissioner under his revisional authhority conferred by section 11(4) is competent to pass an order directing a general revision of allotments.

Pakistan Rehabilitation Rules, 1951, r. 8— Notices may be issued by beat of drum in cases where a large number of persons arc involved, with a view for speedy settlement of question, especially Where proposed revision is on ground common to all— Order of general revision affecting a chak is not an order of a general nature within the meaning of sec.7(1).

Order of general revision affecting a chak was not an order of a “general nature” within meaning of sec. 7(1), Pakistan Rehabilitation Ordinance, 1956.

Abdul Hafiz Vs. Rehabilitation Commr. West Pak. (1966) 18 DLR (SC) 497.
