S. 2—Cyclone Relief Surcharge—Levy of.

By section 2 o1 Ordinance No.1 of 1971 a levy of the Cyclone Relief surcharge was made, Section 3 amended the Income Tax Act and inserted section 3 (b) the charging section for surcharge providing that the relevant Finance Act. o the year shall decide the rates which will be charged for that year in respect of the total income of the previous year or years as the case may be.

M/S. Pak. Jute Mills Ltd. V. Income Tax Officer & another (1979) 31 DLR (AD) 178.
