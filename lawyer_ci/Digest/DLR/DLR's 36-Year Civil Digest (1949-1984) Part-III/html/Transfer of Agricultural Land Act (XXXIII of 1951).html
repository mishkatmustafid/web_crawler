Transfer of Agricultural Land Act (XXXIII of 1951)

 

S. 4— If by specific agreement buyer agrees to take necessary steps for obtaining permission he cannot later on say that ii is seller’s duty to obtain permission.

Purnendu Kumar Das Vs. Hiran Kumar Das, (1969) 21 DLR 918.
