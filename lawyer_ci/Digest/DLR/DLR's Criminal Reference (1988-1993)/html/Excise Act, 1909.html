Excise Act, 1909
 
Section 46—Applicability of the provision relating to search—For the purpose of conducting search in order to find out as to whether a person is guilty of an offence under section 46 of the Excise Act the provision of section 103 CrPC has no application.
Dilip Kumar Ghose Vs. State 42 DLR 464.
