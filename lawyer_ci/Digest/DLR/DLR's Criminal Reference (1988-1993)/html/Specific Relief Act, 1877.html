Specific Relief Act, 1877

 
Specific Relief Act, 1877
[I of 1877]
 
Section 54—
Non consideration of the vital piece of evidence as to injunction granted by the Civil Court—in the face of such an order of injunction no criminal Court can accept beyond reasonable doubt the claim of possession made on oral and other evidence.
Samiruddin Ahmed Vs. State 41 DLR (AD) 129.
