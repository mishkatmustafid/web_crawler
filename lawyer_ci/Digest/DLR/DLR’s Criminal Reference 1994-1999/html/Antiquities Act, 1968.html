Antiquities Act

[XIV of 1968]

 

Section 28—Only a Sessions Court can take cognizance in respect of the offence regarding Antiquities. The cognizance taken by the Special Tribunal, in view of section 28 of the Act, is illegal. Khalilur Rahman (Md) alias Ismail vs State represented by the Deputy Commissioner, Dhaka 51 DLR 344.
