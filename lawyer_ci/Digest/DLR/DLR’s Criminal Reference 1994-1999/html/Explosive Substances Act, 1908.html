Explosive Substances Act

[VI of 1908]

 

Section 5—To constitute offence under the aforesaid sections of law possession and control must mean conscious possession and actual control with necessary mens rea.

When incriminating articles involving offences under section 19(f) of the Arms Act and section 5 of the Explosive Substances Act are recovered from a place in the occupation or possession of more than one person and it is not possible to fix the liability of any particular individual, the head of the family cannot be held liable for the offences. Kashem alias Md Abul Kashem vs State 47 DLR 438.
