Inland Shipping Ordinance

[LXXII of 1976]

Section 47—

Marine Court is constituted only with a Magistrate and the assessors are only to assist the Court by furnishing their opinion and they may remain absent with the permission of the Magistrate concerned. Absence of the assessor will not vitiate the proceedings of the Marine Court.

KM Mahmudur Rahman and others vs State 48 DLR 92.
