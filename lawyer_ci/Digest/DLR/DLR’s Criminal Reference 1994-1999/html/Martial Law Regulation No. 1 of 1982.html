Martial Law Regulation No. 1 of 1982

 

Regulation 3(4)—

When the reviewing authority acted in excess of his power conferred under regulation 3(4) of the Martial Law Regulation No. 1 of 1982 the impugned order passed in reviewing by the CMLA converting the judgment of acquittal into one of conviction is without any lawful authority.

Abdur Rahman vs Bangladesh 49 DLR 344.
