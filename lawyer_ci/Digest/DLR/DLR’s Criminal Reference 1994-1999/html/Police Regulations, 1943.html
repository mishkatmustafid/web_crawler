Police Regulations, 1943

 

Regulation No 280—

The presence of search witnesses must not be looked upon merely as a formality, but they must actually be eye-witnesses to the whole search conducted by the police and must be able to see clearly where each article is found.

Zulfikar Ali @ Kazal vs State 47 DLR 603.
