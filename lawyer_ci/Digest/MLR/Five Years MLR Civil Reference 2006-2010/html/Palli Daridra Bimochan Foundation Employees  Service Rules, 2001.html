Palli Daridra Bimochan Foundation Employees

Service Rules, 2001

 

 Rule   49   (Ka)(Kha)(gha)   and   (cha)-Disciplinary proceedings— General Clauses Act, 1897— Section 6— Legal effect of the repeal of law or rules-Section 6 of the General Clauses Act, 1897 provides that the legal proceedings drawn under certain law or rules shall be continued and disposed of under the same law or rules if the same is repealed or re-enacted during the pendency thereof. This is the settled principle of law. But in the instant case the proceedings were drawn under the BRDB Employees Service Rules, 1988 and the same has been concluded by imposing the penalty of dismissal under the Palli Daridra Bimochan Foundatin Employees Service Rules, 2001 which the learned judges of the High Court Division found illegal and as such declared to have been passed without any lawful authority and of no legal effect. Abdul Kader Mia . Vs. Palli Daridra Bimochan Foundation 11 MLR (2006) (HC) 10.

 

 

Regulation 61(2)— Termination of service of temporary employee and payment of gratuity—•

The authority of the Palli Daridra Bimochan Foundation has the right to terminate the service of temporary employee appointed in a project either on giving three"months notice or payment of three months basic salary. Such provision as contained in Regulation 61(2) is not violative of the fundamental rights. The Appellate Division however held the temporary employees who rendered more than three years service will'be entitled to gratuity. Mir Sattnniddiir and three others Vs. Palli Daridra "Bimochan foundation, represented In/ the Managing Director and another 14 MLR (2009) (AD) 278.
