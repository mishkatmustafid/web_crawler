Registration Act, 1908

 

Section—28(1)

Registration (Amendment) Ordinance, 1985, Section—28(1)

When it is found that a fictitious purchase is effected with the object of providing a legal basis for the registration of a document at a particular Sub-Registrar’s office such a document is to be adjudged as void under Section 28 of the Registration Act.

Hachina Begum Vs. Mosammat Mahfuza Akhter 16 BLD (HCD) 374.

Ref: 6 BLD (AD)56; I4BLD(AD)229;37 DLR(AD) 177—Cited.

 

Section—60

A transfer is complete on entry in the volume under Section 60 of the Registration Act and unless that is done, there is no transfer as contemplated under Section 96 of the State Acquisition and Tenancy Act, 1950. Limitation in case of a proceeding under Section 96 of the Act does not start from the date of execution of the document transferring the land, rather from the date when the document is registered under Section 60 of the Registration Act.

Abdul Majid Vs. Akhil Chandra Sengupta and others, 14 BLD (HCD) 79.

 

Section—60

The date of accrual of the right of preemption is not the date of the execution of the deed of sale or the date of its presentation for registration but it is the date on which it is registered under Section 60 of the Registration Act when title passes effectively.

Dr. Ibrahim Hossain Vs. Mosammat Abeda Khatun and others, 14BLD (HCD) 346.

Ref: Abdul Mutalib Vs. Iman All Mollah and others, 42 DLR (AD) 123; Akhtarun Nessa and another Vs. Habibullah and others, 31 DLR (AD) 88; Azizur Rahman Vs. Bhayetullah and others, 40 DLR (AD) 225: Anwar Hossain and others Vs. Abul Hossain MolIah and others 44 DLR (HCD)79-Cited

 

Section—69

Under section 69 of the Act the Inspector General shall exercise a general superintendence over all the registration offices in Bangladesh and shall have power from time to time to make Rules consistent with the Act.

Government of Bangladesh and ors., Vs Mohammad Faruque, 18 BLD (AD) 276.

Ref: 50 DLR (AD) 27, 13 DLR (SC) 100-Cited.
