Local seizure list witness did not support theprosecution case. But all the persons who are police personnel’s consistentlystated about the recovery of unauthorized fire arms and ammunitions. Arms Act, 1878

Section 19A and 19(f) - Charge of unauthorizedpossession of fire arms can well be proved by evidence of police personnel.

Evidence of police personnel found natural,consistent and reliable can well form the basis of conviction. Evidence ofpolice personnel can not be discarded only because they are police personnel.

Abdus Samad Vs. The State 12 MLR (2007) (HC) 283.

 

Arms Act, 1878

Section 19A and 19(f) - Offence of unauthorizedpossession of fire arms –Proof of charge Sustainability of conviction and sentence.

Contradictions in evidence on material point arefatal to the prosecution. But minor discrepancies of evidence are not fatal andcan be ignored. Evidence of police personnels when found consistent andreliable can well form the basis of conviction. When the convict appellant wascaught red handed after encounter with the police and fire arms were recoveredfrom him and when the charges were established by consistent and reliableevidence the learned judges of the High Court Division upheld the convictionand sentence as perfectly justified.

Ful Mia Vs. The State 13 MLR (2008) (HC) 31.
