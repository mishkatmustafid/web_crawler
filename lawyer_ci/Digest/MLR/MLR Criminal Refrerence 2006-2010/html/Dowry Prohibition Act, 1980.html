Section 4 - Offence of demanding dowry

Though the accused-husband divorcedthe complainant wife on 20.1.1996, the complaint was filed on 13.2.1996 i.ebefore expiry of 90 days when the marriage was still subsisting. On proof ofthe charge for demanding dowry the trial court convicted and sentenced thehusband appellant which the High Court Division as well as the AppellateDivision held perfectly justified.

Shahidullah Kawser (Md.) Vs. IsratZahan Popy and another 13 MLR (2008) (AD) 278.
