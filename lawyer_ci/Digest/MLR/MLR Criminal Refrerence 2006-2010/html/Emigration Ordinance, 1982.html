Emigration Ordinance, 1982

 

Section 23 - Offence of receiving money on the pretext ofproviding foreign employment
The offence under section 23 of the Emigration Ordinance is triable by specialcourt consisting of the Chairman, Labour Court. Such special court can not tryoffence under other law. In the instant case the ingredients of both theoffence under Penal Code and special law are present. The learned judges of theHigh Court Division held that the proceedings either in the special court or inthe ordinary court are maintainable.

Phulbanu alias Phul (Mst.) Vs. TheState 15 MLR (2010) (HC) 332.
