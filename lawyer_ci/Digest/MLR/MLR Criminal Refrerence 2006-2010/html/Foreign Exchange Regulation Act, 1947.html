Section 23 - Proceedings shall have tobe initiated by complaint presented by person duly authorized by the Governmentor the Bangladesh Bank.

Proceedings against contraventions ofany of the provision of the Act, shall have to be initiated on presentation ofcomplaint by a person duly authorized by the Government or the Bangladesh Bank.In the instant case the police lodged the F.I.R and cognizance was taken on thecharge sheet submitted by the police. The Appellate Division held the HighCourt Division was perfectly justified in quashing the proceedings which wereabuse of the process of the court.
