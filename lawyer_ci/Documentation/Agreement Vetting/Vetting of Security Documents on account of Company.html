7.        Creating equitable Mortgage of the Properties Ranking Pari Passu Charges:

In the recital of the above Deed it has been described that the original title deeds shall be deposited to all the four lenders but this is not possible and as such the description shall be like that the Agent shall retain all the original title deeds for itself and on behalf of all other Lenders as a common custodian. Please ensure that all the title deeds no, has been accurately shown in the deed of equitable mortgage and confirm the schedule.

Please note that Mortgage (registered) is to be created in favour of the lenders on the landed property standing in the name of the Borrower Company as such the mortgage deed (Certified copy) is required to be filed with the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies and Firms and the original title deed(s) must be kept with the lenders. Hence the creation of Equitable Mortgage in the above form is not necessary.
