NOW THIS DEED OF AGREEMENT FOR FURTHER CHARGE WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS:

  1. That the MORTGAGORS  have already created the Legal Mortgage in favour of the MORTGAGEE BANK for an amount of Tk.15,00,000.00 (Taka fifteen lac) only allowed by the MORTGAGEE BANK to the BORROWER.
  1. That the MORTGAGORS  hereby undertake and covenant that the Legal Mortgage already created in favour of the MORTGAGEE BANK shall be a continuing security and shall also cover the entire Credit amount of Tk.23,00,000.00 (Taka twenty three lac) only. This deed of agreement for further charge will thereby be applicable for all further sanction, excess drawings or limits exceeding due to application of profits.
  1. That the MORTGAGORS  hereby undertake and covenant that they will not redeem the Legal Mortgage created earlier without first redeeming the equitable mortgage created covering the entire Credit amount of Tk.40,00,000.00 (Taka forty lac) only upon full adjustment of the dues with all other profits and charges thereon.
  1. That if the BORROWER fails to repay the Credit to the MORTGAGEE BANK in the manner stated in the Credit sanction letter or in any other manner so agreed between the BORROWER and the MORTGAGEE BANK, then the MORTGAGEE BANK shall be entitled to recover the outstanding dues from the MORTGAGORS under these presents by sale of the mortgaged properties without intervention of the Court of law.
  1. That the MORTGAGEE BANK, if necessary, shall be entitled to sell the mortgaged properties without intervention of any Court of law at its own discretion as provided in section 69 of the Transfer of Property Act and the same shall be regarded as valid and binding on all the parties, to which the MORTGAGORS  will not object.
  1. That on repayment of the Credit and on payment of all profits and other charges due thereon the MORTGAGORS at their own costs, will be entitled to redeem the mortgaged properties from the MORTGAGEE BANK free from all encumbrances and charges accruing on the MORTGAGEE BANK and on such redemption this mortgage shall be deemed to have been extinguished.
