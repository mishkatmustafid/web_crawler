AND WHEREAS:

The chain of ownership of the Scheduled Property-I is as follows:

It appears that Tarani Chandra Sarkar, Satish Chandra Sarkar, Taher Kha,, Fazlur Rahman Kha, Nicolas Gomes, Gobinda Chandra Roy R. S. recorded owners of some portion of the scheduled land. Thereafter, said (i) Taher Kha & (ii) Nicolas Gomes died leaving behinds their legal heirs including (i) Fazlur Rahman Kha & (ii) Miss Ufregi Gomes (respectively) and they acquired the land by way of succession. Afterwards, said   Tarani Chandra Sarkar, Satish Chandra Sarkar, Fazlur Rahman Kha, Miss Ufregi Gomes, Gobinda Chandra Roy sold the land in favour of Jalal Uddin Khan through (i) Saf Kabala Deed No. 2934 dated 07. 06.1982 (ii) Saf Kabala Deed No. 2935 dated 02. 06.1982 (iii) Saf Kabala Deed No. 5846 dated 06. 10.1983 (iv) Saf Kabala Deed No. 2192 dated 28. 04.1982 & (v) Saf Kabala Deed No. 4750 dated 28.07.1977 (respectively), all registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka.  Then said Jalal Uddin Khan transferred the land measuring 136.50 decimals in favour of Roushan Ara Akter by way of gift through Affidavit bearing Notarial Certificate No. 22 dated 13. 01.1990.

On the other hand, Robjel Uddin Ahammod was the R. S. recorded owner of another portion of the scheduled land. Thereafter, said Robjel Uddin Ahammod sold the land measuring 22 (twenty two) decimals in favour of Roushan Ara Akter through the Saf Kabala Deed No. 3700 dated 12.07.1984, registered with Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka.

On the other hand, Zabeda Khatun was the owner of another portion of the scheduled land and she acquired the land by way of purchase through the Saf Kabala Deed No. 7880 dated 30.10.1974, registered with Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka. Thereafter, said Zabeda Khatun  transferred the land measuring 23 (twenty three) decimals in favour of Roushan Ara Akter by way of gift through Heba Bel Ewaz Deed No. 4461 dated 17.11.1998,  registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka.

Thus the said Roushan Ara Akter has become the present owner of the scheduled land measuring (136.50+22+23) = 181.50 decimals & got mutated the land measuring 161 (one hundred sixty one) decimals 163 decimalsout of 181.50 decimals in the govt. revenue record vide mutation case no, 2262/05-06 dated 30.01.2006, as per R. S. operation & paying ground rent thereof.

The chain of ownership of the Scheduled Property-II is as follows:

It appears that Radhika Lal Shaha & Khirod Chandra Shaha were the S. A. recorded owners of the scheduled land along with other land. Thereafter, said Radhika Lal Shaha sold the land in favour of (i) Kazi Yakub Ali (ii) Kazi Lutfor Rahman (iii) Kazi Mobarok Hossain (iv) Kazi Mosharrof Hossain through Saf Kabala Deed Nos. 5411 & 5412, both dated 26. 07.1969 and registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka. Then said (i) Kazi Yakub Ali (ii) Kazi Lutfor Rahman (iii) Kazi Mobarok Hossain (iv) Kazi Mosharrof Hossain & (v) Khirod Chandra Shaha recorded their names in R. S. operation. Thereafter, said Khirod Chandra Shaha sold his portion of the land in favour of (i) Kazi Yakub Ali (ii) Kazi Lutfor Rahman (iii) Kazi Mobarok Hossain (iv) Kazi Mosharrof Hossain through Saf Kabala Deed No. 4424 dated 25. 06.1974, registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka. Later on, said Kazi Mobarok Hossain sold his portion of the land in favour of Kazi Yakub Ali through Saf Kabala Deed No. 5066 dated 28.08.1983, registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka. After that, said Kazi Yakub Ali partitioned the land with (i) Kazi Lutfor Rahman & (ii) Kazi Mosharrof Hossain through Partition Deed No. 699 dated 04.03.1986, registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka. Subsequently, said Kazi Yakub Ali died leaving behind his legal heirs one wife, two sons and three daughters namely (i) Roushan Ara Akter (ii) Kazi Abdur Rashid (iii) Kazi Arham Uddin (iv) Kazi Bithi Rahman (v) Fahmida Ali & (vi) Kazi Farhana Yasmin and  they acquired the land by way of succession. Then said Kazi Bithi Rahman appointed Kazi Abdur Rashid as her constituted attorney for her porton of the land through General Power of Attorney Deed No. 388 dated 18.01.2012, registered with the Nawabgonj Sub-Registry Office of Dhaka.

S. operation & paying ground rent thereof.
