In such situation, Bank may obtain a registered Agreement for Sale executed by and between the borrower Mr. Azadul Islam and the landowner clearly mentioning the time for transferring the apartment along with undivided and undemarcated land by way of a Deed of Sale. Upon obtaining mandatory wanting documents/papers as mentioned clause 13 above.
